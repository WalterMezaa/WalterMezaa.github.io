Record Samsung Galaxy A13 Intent This step-by-step tutorial will show you how to screen record on your Samsung Galaxy A13 device.

Step 1: Access Quick Settings Open the Notification Shade by swiping down vertically from the top of your Samsung Galaxy A13 device to expose the “Quick Settings” options.

Step 2: Find Screen Record The Screen Record icon looks like a circle with a dot in the center and two arrows in the corners. Tap on this icon and it will open the Screen Record Settings.

Step 3: Set Up Screen Recording Screen Recording can be set up to record in portrait or landscape mode. You can also choose to record audio from the device microphone as well as the audio from any connected headset.

Step 4: Start Recording When you are ready to start recording, tap the “Record” button. You’ll be given a three second countdown before the recording starts.

Step 5: Stop Recording Once you are done recording, tap the “Stop” button on the bottom right. Your recording will automatically be saved to the Gallery.

Step 6: Share If you would like to share your recording, open the Gallery and choose the video you want to share. You can then share it via email, social media or other methods.

1. Overview of Samsung Galaxy A13 Screen Recorder#

Screen recording is simple to do with the Samsung Galaxy A13. To begin, open the Notification Shade by swiping down from the top of your screen. You’ll find the Screen Record icon (circle with dot and two arrows) along the Quick Actions in the upper right corner. Tap this icon to start the recording. You’ll see a three-second countdown on your screen, and then the recording will begin. When your recording is complete, you can tap the stop button in the upper right corner of your screen and the recording will be saved in your Gallery.

Additionally, you can customize the length of your recordings by tapping the Settings icon (cog wheel) and adjusting the maximum recording time. The maximum recording time default is 5 minutes, but you can increase it up to 10 minutes. You also have the option to record with sound or without sound. You can switch back and forth between the two options before every recording.

You also have the option to draw on your recordings while they are being recorded. This can be useful if you’re demonstrating how to use a certain app or website. Tap the Pen icon, then select the color and pen size you’d like to use for your drawing. When you’re finished, tap the Stop button and your recording will be saved to your Gallery.

2. Checklist to Performing Samsung Galaxy A13 Screen Record#

  1. Start by swiping down from the top of the home screen to open the Notification Shade.
  2. Scroll down to find the Screen Record icon, which is a circle with a dot and two arrows.
  3. Tap the icon to begin the recording. You will see a countdown of three seconds so you can prepare.
  4. Once it starts, the time will be displayed at the top of the screen. The record button will appear at the right side of the notification bar if you wish to stop the recording.
  5. To pause the recording, tap the pause button.
  6. If you do not want to save the recording, simply tap “Stop” in the notification shade. If you wish to keep it, tap “Save”.
  7. You can find the saved recording in your Gallery app.

3. Guideline to Screen Record on Samsung Galaxy A13#

  1. Firstly, you will need to open the Notification Shade by swiping down from the top of the display.
  2. Once you opened the Notification Shade, you can then look for the Screen Record icon which appears as a circle with a dot and two arrows.
  3. Once you find the icon, you simply need to tap on it to start recording.
  4. You also have the option to record with sound if you want to. You can do this by tapping on the sound icon on the upper right corner of the Screen Record card.
  5. Before you start recording, you can also adjust the length of the recording by tapping on the Timer icon on the upper left corner of the Screen Record card.
  6. Once everything is set and you are ready to record, simply hit the Start icon to begin recording.

4. Tips & Troubleshooting for Samsung Galaxy A13 Screen Record#

  1. To screen record on the Samsung Galaxy A13, start by pulling the Notification Shade down to access the Quick Settings menu.
  2. Look for the Screen Record icon, which is a circle with one dot and two arrows.
  3. Tap the Screen Record icon and it will start recording automatically.
  4. To end the recording you can either tap on the Stop icon in the Notification shade or press the Power button.
  5. If you want to add a comment to your recording, you can press the Microphone button before you start.
  6. You can find all your recordings in the Gallery app.
  7. For audio recording during your screen recordings, you can enable the Speaker Option in the Screen Recording settings.
