Overall, screen recording on the Samsung Galaxy A13 is easy to do, and it gives you a great way to capture activities on your device. With the ability to customize the settings and use a timer, you can ensure that you record exactly what you need without missing any essential elements.

3. Problems You May Encounter While Recording Screen on Samsung Galaxy A13#

  1. One issue you may have while recording your screen on Samsung Galaxy A13 is that your device’s display may lag while you’re recording, resulting in a stuttering video if you have too many applications running at the same time.

  2. You may also encounter an error when attempting to record your device’s screen if you are trying to screen record a game or app that does not permit the recording of its content.

  3. If you are using an older version of Android, you may find that the device record feature is not available due to compatibility issues. To ensure that your device can record screen properly, you will need to update your device’s OS to the latest version.

  4. Your device may also refuse to record screen activity if you have exceeded the maximum recording time, which is usually 3 minutes. In this case, you will need to restart the recording and split it into shorter segments if needed.

  5. If you find that your recording audio is not clear, ensure that the Audio Source is set to ‘Device Audio’ in the Quick Settings.

  6. Finally, if your recordings lag or appear distorted, you may need to reduce the recording resolution settings in the Quick Settings to ensure that your device can handle the recording load.
