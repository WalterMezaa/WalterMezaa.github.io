2. Learn How to Take Screenshots on Nokia 7.2 Running Android 11#

  1. The first step when taking screenshots on a Nokia 7.2 running Android 11 is to open the desired application.

  2. Next, press the Power Button and Volume Down Button at the same time for a few seconds.

  3. After pressing both buttons, you should hear a shutter sound or a notification indicating that the screenshot has been taken.

  4. This screenshot will automatically be saved in the device’s Gallery.

  5. Nokia 7.2 also offers a few additional screenshot settings. To access these settings go into Settings > System > Advanced > Screenshots and Screen Recording.

  6. From here, you can choose to enable or disable the shutter sound, or designate a screenshot folder for all your screenshots.

  7. With these simple steps, you can quickly and easily take screenshots on a Nokia 7.2 running Android 11.

3. Tips and Tricks for Taking Screenshots on Nokia 7.2#

  1. Swipe down from the top of the screen and click the Capture+ icon in the Quick Settings panel.

  2. Alternatively, you can also take a screenshot by pressing and holding the Power button and Volume Down button together for a few seconds.

  3. When you hear a shutter sound or see a notification, your screenshot is ready. In the notification panel, you will find the Capture+ icon which will open the screenshot.

  4. The screenshot will also be saved in your phone’s Gallery, in the Screenshots folder. You can open it from there and share it or edit it.

  5. You can also take a screenshot of the whole page by swiping up (or down if you are on the Display Settings page) and pressing the Capture+ icon in the Quick Settings panel.

  6. Furthermore, Nokia 7.2 also has a feature called “Scroll Capture”. This feature allows you to capture multiple screenshots of a webpage and combine them into a single image. To use this feature, open the Capture+ icon in the Quick Settings panel, tap Scroll Capture, and then swipe up or down to capture the entire page.
