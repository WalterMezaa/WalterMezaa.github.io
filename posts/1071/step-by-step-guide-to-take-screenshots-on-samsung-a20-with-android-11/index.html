How to Capture Screenshots on Samsung A20 with Android 11

  1. Open the desired screen you want to capture.
  2. Press the power button and the volume down button of Samsung A20 at the same time.
  3. Hold the buttons for about 1-2 seconds until the screenshot is successfully captured.
  4. A screenshot captured sound will be hear and a notification will be shown.
  5. The Screenshot will be automatically saved in the Gallery of the Samsung A20.
  6. To access the screenshot, go to the Gallery and choose “Screenshots” folder.
  7. You can view, share or edit the screenshot you just captured.

2. Methods to Take Screenshots on Samsung A20 with Android 11#

  1. Make sure the Samsung A20 has the latest update of Android 11.
  2. To take a quick screenshot, press the Power and Volume Down buttons of your device simultaneously for about 1-2 seconds.
  3. You will hear a sound and see a notification once the screenshot is captured.
  4. The screenshot is automatically saved in the Gallery, which can be accessed through the Apps menu.
  5. You can also access the screenshot directly from the notification panel.
  6. To view, edit, and share the screenshot, go to the Gallery app.

3. How to Take Long Screenshots on Samsung A20 with Android 11#

If you want to take a long screenshot, press and hold the Power and Volume Down buttons of the Samsung A20 for 1-2 seconds. After the screenshot captured sound is heard and the notification is displayed, a Scroll Capture icon will appear in the screenshot preview. Tap it to start scrolling and capture more contents. To capture different screens, just tap the captured screens. When the screenshot is complete, tap the Done button. The long screenshot will also be saved in the Gallery and can be accessed in the same way as the normal screenshots.

4. Tips for Taking Good Quality Screenshots on Samsung A20 with Android 11#

Besides pressing the two buttons to capture the screenshot, you can also use the Bixby voice command feature. To do this, long press the Bixby button and simply say “take a screenshot”. You will hear a notification sound confirming that the screenshot has been taken, and the screenshot will be automatically saved in the Gallery. Another way you can take screenshots is by swiping the edge of your hand across the screen. This is the palm swipe gesture feature. To enable it, go to Settings > Advanced features > Motions and Gestures > Palm swipe to capture. Once enabled, you can take screenshots by simply swiping the edge of your hand across the screen. You will also hear a notification sound confirming that the screenshot has been taken, and the screenshot will be automatically saved in the Gallery.
