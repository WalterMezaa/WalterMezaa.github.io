4. Suggest ways to share the screenshot#

Once the screenshot is taken, it will be saved in your notification tray. To access it, simply open the notification tray by swiping down from the top of the screen. From there, tap the screenshot preview to open it and share or delete it.

You can share the screenshot by tapping the share button at the bottom right corner of the screenshot. You can then save or share the screenshot to any app on your device.

Moreover, you can also save the screenshot directly on the external storage. To do this, long press the screenshot preview in the notification tray and tap “save as”. Alternatively, you can also open the screenshot in the gallery; from there, you can share, save, and delete it.

When sharing, you can directly send the screenshot via any app of your choice, such as messages, email or social media platforms. Lastly, you can also save the screenshot to cloud storage services, such as Google Drive, and share the link with your contacts.
