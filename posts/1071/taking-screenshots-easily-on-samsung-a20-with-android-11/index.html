Take Screenshot on A20

How to Capture Screenshots on Samsung A20 with Android 11 The Samsung A20 with Android 11 makes it easy for you to take a screenshot anytime. This tutorial explains how to do it quickly and easily.

  1. First, locate the content you want to capture on your home screen.

  2. Press the Power button and Volume Down key simultaneously.

  3. You will see an animation and hear a shutter sound, this indicates that a screenshot has been taken.

  4. Your screenshot will appear as a thumbnail in the lower right corner of the screen which you can open and share or delete.

  5. Alternatively, you can take a screenshot by swiping three fingers on the screen.

  6. Finally, if you want to quickly access your screenshots you can open the “Gallery” app and select “Screenshots” to view all the screenshots you have taken.

2. Step-by-Step Guide on Taking Screenshot on Samsung A20 with Android 11#

First off, you need to go to your Samsung A20 and make sure that it is running on Android 11. Then long press the Power Button and Volume Down at the same time for about 2 seconds. The animation will appear on the screen and you’ll hear the shutter sound. Now your screenshot is taken and you can save it or share it with your friends.

You can view your screenshot in the Gallery app. Just open the app and go to the Screenshots folder. You will find your screenshot there that you can view or save.

If you’d rather share it directly without saving it, you can use the Share button in the Gallery app. Just hit the Share button, select which apps you’d like to share it with and you’re done. The apps you select will now receive the screenshot.

In addition, you can also take scrolling screenshots on your Samsung A20 with Android 11. Just take a normal screenshot as described above. Now when you open the screenshot in the Gallery app, you’ll find a Scroll Capture button. Click on that and the phone will start taking a screenshot of the whole page.

Moreover, you can also edit your screenshots. Select the screenshot you want to edit from the Gallery, and then hit the Edit button. Here you have a range of tools to customize your screenshot the way you want.

Lastly, you can also take screenshots with the assistant menu. Scroll down the notification tray and open the assistant menu. Here you’ll see a Screenshot button. Just tap it and the screenshot will be taken.

Q1. How to take a screenshot on a Samsung A20 with Android 11? A1. To take a screenshot with a Samsung A20 with Android 11, you can press and hold the power button and volume down button simultaneously. Q2. Can I take scrolling screenshots on Samsung A20? A2. Yes, you can take scrolling screenshots on Samsung A20 with Android 11. You can do this by pressing and holding the power button and volume down button to take the first screenshot, and then pressing and holding the power button and volume down button again to continue the scrolling screenshot.

Q3. Are there any other ways to take screenshots on Samsung A20? A3. Yes, you can also take screenshots on Samsung A20 with Android 11 using the quick settings panel. To do this, simply swipe down from the top of the screen and tap on the Screenshot icon.

Q4. How do you crop screenshots on a Samsung A20? A4. To crop screenshots on a Samsung A20 with Android 11, simply open the screenshot and then tap on the crop icon at the bottom. This will allow you to crop the screenshot to your desired size.

Q5. Can I edit screenshots on Samsung A20? A5. Yes, you can edit screenshots on Samsung A20 with Android 11. After taking a screenshot, simply tap on the edit icon at the bottom and you can use the various editing tools to make changes to the screenshot.
