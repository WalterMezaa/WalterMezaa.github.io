Step by Step Guide to Take Screenshots on Samsung A20 with Android 11 Intent This complete guide will show you how to take a screenshot on a Samsung A20 with Android 11.

Step 1: Locate the power and volume button On the right side of the phone, you will find the power button and the volume buttons.

Step 2: Press the power and volume down buttons Press the power button and the volume down button simultaneously. This will take a screenshot of the current screen.

Step 3: Access the screenshot Once you have taken the screenshot, you will find it in your notification bar. You can also access it in your photo gallery.

Step 4: Edit the screenshot If you want to edit the screenshot, you can use your phone’s built-in editing options. You can crop, resize, or even add text or draw on the screenshot.

Step 5: Share the screenshot Once you have edited the screenshot, you can share it with friends or family. You can share it on social media, send it via text message, or even email it.

1. Overview of Samsung A20 with Android 11#

Samsung A20 with Android 11 is the latest iteration of the popular A series of smartphones. The new device is equipped with higher-resolution displays, an improved processor, and the latest Android operating system. Taking screenshots on a Samsung A20 with Android 11 is quick and easy. All you need to do is press and hold the power and volume down buttons together to capture the current screen. The screenshot will be saved to your gallery automatically and can be shared with friends or family. You can also take screenshots directly from the Notification Panel by tapping on the screenshot icon. Finally, if you want to take a long screenshot, the scrolling screenshot feature makes it easy to capture a multi-page document or web page.

2. Steps to Take a Screenshot#

  1. To enable taking screenshots with any button combination on Samsung A20 with Android 11, swipe down the notification panel.

  2. Tap ‘Settings’ and select ‘Advanced Features’ on the resulting screen.

  3. Then select ‘Motions and Gestures’ from the options displayed on the screen.

  4. Now select ‘Palm swipe to capture’.

  5. Toggle the switch to enable this feature.

  6. To take a screenshot, swipe the side of your hand across the screen from left to right or from right to left.

  7. Alternatively, pressing the power and volume down buttons together for a couple of seconds will also capture the current screen.

  8. The screenshot will be saved in the default ‘Screenshot’ folder located in the gallery app.

  9. Users can also view, edit, and share their screenshots directly from the notification panel.

  10. With Android 11, Samsung A20 also provides its users with an improved screenshot editing tool. This tool enables users to quickly edit their screenshots and crop, highlight, or add text to them.

  11. Lastly, users can easily share their screenshots with other users via direct messaging or social media platforms.

3. Additional Screenshot Features#

  1. For more accuracy, you can press and hold the power button to bring up the ‘Take Screenshot’ option and then select it.
  2. Another interesting feature of the Samsung A20 is the Screenshot Preview. Once you have taken a screenshot, a preview will appear at the top right hand corner of the screen.
  3. You can also use palm swipe gesture to take screenshots in Samsung A20 with Android 11. To use this feature, swipe the side of your hand across the screen from left to right or right to left.
  4. In Samsung A20 you can also take scrolling screenshots which enable you to capture a larger portion of the screen than conventional screenshots. To take scrolling screenshots, you need to press and hold the ‘Capture’ button after taking a screenshot.
  5. You can also add annotations or drawings to your screenshots before saving or sharing them. The screenshot preview will give you the option to directly draw or annotate your screenshots before saving.
  6. Lastly, you can also edit screenshots before sharing or saving them. Once you have taken the screenshot, Samsung A20 with Android 11 will give you the option to crop, rotate or adjust the brightness and colour of your screenshots.

4. Conclusions#

Once you have taken a screenshot, it is stored in the Gallery application under Screenshots folder. To delete any screenshot, open the Gallery application, select the screenshot and delete it. You can also share the screenshot directly by selecting the Share option from the Gallery application. Moreover, you can share the screenshot directly from the Notification panel also.
