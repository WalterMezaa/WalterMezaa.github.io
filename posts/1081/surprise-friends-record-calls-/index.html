Next, you’ll want to enable the call recording feature on your Android phone. To do this, go to the Settings menu of your device and select the “Phone” tab. Scroll down until you find the “Call Recording” option and enable it. Once the feature is enabled, all calls will be automatically recorded.

For added security, some apps also have the option to set up a PIN or password for the call recordings. This is recommended so that only you can access and listen to the recordings. This feature also helps keep recorded calls secure in case someone else were to get their hands on your device.

Another great feature provided by some of these apps is the ability to save your recordings directly to the cloud. This eliminates the need for manual backup and lets you access the recordings from any device.

Finally, you can also customize the recording settings to meet your preference. You can choose to record incoming calls only, outgoing calls only, or both. You can also specify how long the recording should last, as well as to what format the recording should be saved.

The next step is to configure the app to automatically delete the recordings after a certain period of time, such as one week or one month. This will ensure that your recordings are always up to date and not taking up too much space on your device. Additionally, you can set the app to automatically delete recordings after a certain number of days, which is a great way to save valuable storage space.

The recording process itself is simple. When making a call, a small red recording button will appear on the screen.
