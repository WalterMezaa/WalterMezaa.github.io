How to Take a Screenshot on Windows 10 Using Shortcut Method

Tired of searching for ways to take a screenshot on Windows 10? Look no further! Here we will show you a shortcut method to get the job done quickly and easily. Screenshots are a great way to capture what’s on your computer screen and can be a useful tool in many situations. Taking a screenshot on Windows 10 can be done quickly and easily using the shortcut method. In this article, we’ll show you how to take a screenshot on Windows 10 using the easiest and quickest way possible.

  1. Taking a screenshot on Windows 10 is incredibly easy, especially when using the shortcut method. According to PCWorld, the shortcut method is the most commonly used way to take screenshots on Windows 10. It involves pressing the “Windows key” + “Print Screen” at the same time. This will capture an image of your entire display and save it to the Pictures > Screenshots folder.

  2. According to Microsoft, saving a screenshot with the shortcut method also captures the entire screen and copies it to the clipboard. You can then paste it into any program, such as Word, Paint or email. This makes it easy to quickly share a screenshot with others.

  3. Lastly, the shortcut method also provides a number of options to customize your screenshot. You can choose whether to take a screen capture of the entire display or just a single window, and can also choose to add annotation and highlighting. This makes taking screenshots a breeze and helps to ensure that each image captures exactly what you want it to.

Take Screenshots Windows 10 To take a screenshot of your Windows 10 screen using the shortcut method, follow these steps:

  1. Press the ‘Windows’ key + ‘Shift’ + ‘S’ simultaneously on your keyboard.
  2. Your screen will appear darker and your mouse cursor will change.
  3. Click and drag your mouse cursor to select the area of the screen that you want to capture as a screenshot.
  4. Once you have selected the area, release the mouse button and a screenshot of the selected area will be saved to the clipboard.
  5. You can then paste the screenshot into a text editor, image editor, or any other program.

1. Preparation of tools and materials#

Firstly, you will need to press ‘Windows’ + ‘Shift’ + ‘S’ keys simultaneously to make the screen go dark and the cursor will change. Secondly, choose the area of the screen you want to capture and then hold and drag the cursor over the selected area. Once you are happy with the area, release the mouse and the screenshot will be taken. Thirdly, the screenshot will be saved in your clipboard, which you can paste into any image or document tool. Lastly, you can save the screenshot as an image file, in formats such as JPG or PNG.

2. Activating the keyboard shortcut#

  1. On your keyboard, press the Windows key, the Shift key and the S key at the same time.
  2. When the shortcut is activated, the Windows screen will go dark and the cursor will change.
  3. Now you can select the area of the screen that you would like to capture. Simply click the left mouse button and drag the cursor to the desired area.
  4. When you have selected the correct area, release the left mouse button.
  5. The screen will go back to normal and a copy of the image has been saved in the clipboard.
  6. To paste the image in another program, press ‘Control’ + ‘V’ simultaneously. The image will be pasted in the program.

3. Taking a screenshot#

After pressing the combination of ‘Windows’, ‘Shift’ and ‘S’ keys simultaneously, the screen of your Windows 10 will go dark and the cursor of your mouse will change. The mouse cursor will turn into a crosshair icon, which you can use to select the area of the screen that you want to capture as a screenshot. When you have selected the area that you want to capture, take your finger off the left mouse button to take the screenshot. Once the screenshot is taken, it will be saved to your clipboard. All you need to do next is open a program like Paint or Photoshop and paste it in.

4. Save and finalize the screenshot#

Once the screenshot is taken, it is stored in your clipboard. You can paste it in any application you want, like an image editor or a document. To ensure that your screenshot is saved permanently, you may also save it in your computer’s file system or cloud storage. Go to File -> Save As, and give the screenshot a name and choose the file format. Then select the location where you want to save it, click Save and your screenshot is ready for use. Additionally, you can also press the ‘Ctrl’ + ‘S’ keys simultaneously to save the screenshot. Finally, you can also open the screenshot in the Windows Photos application, where you can further customize it and share it with other people.

Q: How do I find the screenshot I just took? A: The screenshot you just took will be saved to the Pictures > Screenshots folder. You can access this folder by going to the Start menu and then clicking on “Pictures” in the list of options.

Q: Can I save the screenshot to a different location? A: Yes, you can save your screenshot to a different location if desired. Simply press the Windows logo + PrtScn, then click the “Copy” button in the bottom-right of the window that appears. Next, open the folder or application where you want to save the screenshot. Then, press Ctrl + V to save the screenshot.

Q: Is there any other way to take a screenshot on Windows 10? A: Yes, you can also take a screenshot on Windows 10 by using the Snipping Tool. This is a free application included with Windows that allows you to select an area of the screen to capture. The screenshot will be saved to your clipboard and you can then paste it wherever you like.

Q: What if I want to take a screenshot of just a single window? A: To take a screenshot of a single window, press Alt + PrtScn. This will capture the active window and save the image to your clipboard. You can then paste it into the application or folder of your choice.
