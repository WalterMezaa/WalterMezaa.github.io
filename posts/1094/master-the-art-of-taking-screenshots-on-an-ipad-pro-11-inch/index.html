- Step 1: Preparing the iPad Pro 11 Inch#

Step 1: Preparing the iPad Pro 11 Inch. Before you start taking screenshots, make sure that you have all the necessary tools such as the iPad Pro 11 Inch. On the iPad, go to “Settings” and select “General”, and then select “Accessibility”. In the Accessibility settings, scroll down and select “AssistiveTouch”. Turn on the “AssistiveTouch” feature. This will enable the on-screen Home and Volume buttons.

Step 2: Locate the Home and Volume buttons. The Home and Volume buttons are located on the side of the iPad Pro 11 Inch. The Home button is located at the top right corner of the device and the Volume up button is located at the bottom right corner.

Step 3: Press the Home and Volume up buttons. In order to take a screenshot on the iPad Pro 11 Inch, you just need to press and hold the Home and Volume up buttons at the same time. Make sure that you press and hold them until you hear a beep.

Step 4: Find and view the screenshot. Once you have taken the screenshot, it will be saved in the Screenshots folder in the Photos app. You can open the Photos app to view and edit your screenshot.

Step 5: Share the screenshot. You can share the screenshot with other people by selecting the screenshot in the Photos app and tapping the share icon. You can then choose the app or method you want to use to share your screenshot.

- Step 2: Learn the Screenshot Button Combinations#

Step 2: Learn the Screenshot Button Combinations : Taking screenshots on your iPad Pro 11 Inch is easy and only requires two simple steps. You must press the Home button and the Volume up button simultaneously to capture the moment on your iPad Pro 11 Inch. To ensure that the screenshot is successful, both the Home and Volume up buttons should be held down for at least a second before release. This will allow for the proper capture of the desired image. Furthermore, once a successful screenshot is taken, your iPad Pro 11 Inch will generate a notification to confirm the action. With this in mind, you can confidently take screenshots knowing that your desired image has been successfully captured.

- Step 3: Editing and Saving the Screenshot#

Step 3: Editing and Saving the Screenshot. Once you’ve taken your screenshot and it appears on your iPad Pro 11 Inch screen, you can tap on it to edit and save it. - Step 4: Sharing Your Screenshot to Others#

Step 4: Sharing Your Screenshot to Others. After taking a screenshot, it can be shared to other people by selecting the Share button found at the lower-left corner of the screenshot previewer. Choose the app or service you want to share with and you are good to go. You can share your screenshots with any apps that can share content such as Mail, Notes, Message, or a third-party app like Slack and Dropbox. You can also share your screenshots directly from the Photos app, just tap on the screenshot, select the share button, and then follow the same steps as above.
