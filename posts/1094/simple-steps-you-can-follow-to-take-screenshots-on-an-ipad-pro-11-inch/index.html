  6. The screenshot will be saved directly into the Photos app.

  8. To share the screenshot, tap on the screenshot and select the method you want to use to share it.

4. How to Access to Your Screenshots on iPad Pro 11 Inch#

To access your screenshots on the iPad Pro 11 Inch, open the Photos app. Your screenshots are saved in the Screenshots album, which you can find in the Albums tab at the bottom of the Photos menu. To view your screenshots on the Photos app, select the Albums tab and scroll to the Screenshots album. This album will contain all the screenshots you have taken on your device. You can view each screenshot by tapping on individual thumbnails and can share them to other apps by tapping the share button. You can also delete screenshots by selecting delete in the bottom corner of the thumbnail and confirm the deletion of the screenshot by tapping on delete again.
