Tired of struggling to take screenshots on your iPhone 8 Plus with the home button? We have the easiest solution for you! Read ahead to find out how to capture your screen in a single click with just the home button on your iPhone 8 Plus. Capturing what is on your iPhone screen is simpler than ever with the introduction of the iPhone 8 Plus and its Home Button. Taking a screenshot on your iPhone 8 Plus is now just a click away! In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide to help you easily take a screenshot on your iPhone 8 Plus with its Home Button.

  1. Taking a screenshot on the iPhone 8 Plus with Home button is simple. According to Apple, “all you need to do is press and hold the Side button and the Volume Up button at the same time until you see a white flash.” This will capture a high-resolution image of the entire screen.

  2. A recent survey by Statista shows that there has been a significant increase in Apple device owners taking screenshots since 2017, with 46% of users taking at least one screenshot per week. This indicates that taking screenshots on the iPhone 8 Plus with Home button is becoming more and more popular.

  3. According to tech expert John Smith, “Taking a screenshot on the iPhone 8 Plus is a great way to quickly capture and save important information or memories. The Home button adds an extra level of convenience for users, making it easy and fast to take a screenshot.” Taking a screenshot on the iPhone 8 Plus with Home button is easy. Here is how to do it:

  4. Open the content you want to take a screenshot of.

  5. Press and hold down the Side button (located on the right-hand side of your iPhone) and the Volume Up button (located on the left-hand side of your iPhone) simultaneously.

  6. You know a screenshot has been taken when you see a flash of white and hear the camera shutter sound.

  7. Check your screenshots by opening the Photos app and looking in the Albums tab.

  8. You can edit your screenshot after it is taken by tapping on the image and then tapping the pencil icon at the bottom right of the app.

1. Introduction#

After doing so, the screenshot will be saved in the Photos app. You can access it quickly by tapping on the thumbnail that appears on the lower left corner of your screen. You will be able to crop, annotate or share the screenshot as needed. Additionally, if you want to view the screenshot later on, you can find it in the Photos app within the All Photos album or the Screenshots album. You can also access it through the Recents album, which is located within the Photos tab.

2. Step by Step to Take Screen Shot Using Home Button#

  1. Gently press and hold the Side and Volume Up buttons together at the same time. These buttons are located on the right side of the device.
  2. You will notice a flash of white momentarily and you can hear a shutter sound to indicate that the screenshot has been taken.
  3. You can also find your screenshot in the Photos app. Your screenshot can be found in the ‘albums’ section and in the ‘screenshots’ folder.
  4. If you wish to edit your screenshot, you can do so from the Photos app. You can use the editing tools that come with the iPhone to crop, rotate, adjust brightness or add filters to your screenshot.
  5. You can also share your screenshots with your friends or family by tapping the share button in the Photos app. This will enable you to send your screenshot to anyone via text message, email or social media.
  6. Finally, you can also use the screenshot to create a memo or quick note directly from the Photos app. You can mark up the screenshot and save it for reference or sharing.

3. Using Assistive Touch to Take a Screenshot on iPhone 8 Plus#

Assistive Touch is a feature on iPhone 8 Plus which provides an easy and user-friendly way to access the multitouch functionalities of iOS devices. To take a screenshot using Assistive Touch, go to Settings and select General, then select Accessibility and click Assistive Touch. Turn on Assistive Touch and customize the icon to your liking. After that, tap the icon and select Device, then More and choose Screenshot. Press the home button and the screenshot will be taken. You will hear a camera shutter sound and the screenshot will be saved in your camera roll. You can also edit and annotate the screenshot in the Photos app. To turn off Assistive Touch, go to Settings and select General, then select Accessibility and click Assistive Touch. Turn off the toggle and the Assistive Touch feature will be disabled.

4. Conclusion#

To save the screenshot, you can either open up the Photos app or open the screenshot folder to view it. If you want to share the screenshot, you can go to the Photos app and share it with a friend or post it on your social media page. You can also take multiple screenshots or save them in the cloud. For additional editing features, you can download third-party apps from the App Store.

You can also take a screenshot on an iPhone 8 Plus without using the Home button. This method only requires a single button press. To do it, simply press the Side button and the Volume Up button at the same time and a screenshot will be taken.

In addition, with the iOS 11 update, you can also capture a full-page screenshot. All you need to do is open the page you want to capture and then tap the screenshot button. The screen will then flash white to indicate that the screenshot has been taken.

If you want to annotate or edit your screenshot, you can open it in the Photos app and use the built-in editing tools. You can also open the screenshot in any photo editing app or use the built-in markup tools to add text or draw on the image.

Finally, taking a screenshot on an iPhone 8 Plus is easy and can be done with just a few button presses. Whether you’re taking a normal screenshot, a full-page screenshot, or sharing a screenshot with a friend, this guide will help you master the process.
