Q: What is the easiest way to take a screenshot on an iPhone 8 Plus? A: The easiest way to take a screenshot on an iPhone 8 Plus is to quickly press the Sleep/Wake (power) button on the right side of the phone and the Home button located at the bottom of the phone at the same time.

Q: Where can I find the screenshot I have taken with my iPhone 8 Plus? A: The screenshot you have taken with your iPhone 8 Plus can be found in the Photos app. Go to the Photos app, tap on the Albums tab and then select the Screenshots album.

Q: Is there a shortcut way to open my screenshots on iPhone 8 Plus? A: Yes, there is a shortcut way to open your screenshots on iPhone 8 Plus. Go to the Photos app, 3D Touch (press and hold down) the Screenshots album and you will see all the screenshots stored in the album.

Q: Are there any editing options for screenshots taken with iPhone 8 Plus? A: Yes, you can mark up and edit screenshots taken with your iPhone 8 Plus. Open the screenshot, tap on the Edit button located at the top and you will be able to add text, arrows, magnifying glass, spotlight and more to the screenshot.

Q: Are screenshots taken with iPhone 8 Plus automatically saved to iCloud? A: No, screenshots taken with iPhone 8 Plus are not automatically saved to iCloud. You have to manually enable the iCloud Photos option to sync your screenshots to iCloud. Go to Settings > Photos & Camera and tap on iCloud Photos to enable the feature.
