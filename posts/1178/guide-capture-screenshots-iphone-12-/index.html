1. Introduction to iPhone 12#

The iPhone 12 is the latest Apple smartphone that comes with the latest iOS 14. It is powered by a new A14 Bionic Chip and has a Super Retina XDR Display. It is thinner than the other iPhones and is available in different colors and capacities. Taking a screenshot on an iPhone 12 is easy and fun. Here is a quick guide on how to do it.

First, locate the Volume Up & Power buttons. They are located on the right side of the phone, near the top. Make sure that both buttons are pressed at the same time.

Next, press both buttons simultaneously. You will know you have taken a screenshot when you hear a camera shutter sound or see a flash on the screen. The screenshot will then be saved in the Photos app.

You can also take a screenshot by using AssistiveTouch. To do this, open Settings > Accessibility > Touch and then enable AssistiveTouch. When enabled, a small circle icon will appear on the screen. Touch the icon and tap the “Device” option. Select “More” and then “Screenshot” to save the screenshot.

If you want to share a screenshot you have taken, you can do that too. Simply open the screenshot in the Photos app and tap the “Share” button. You can then select the app you want to use to share the screenshot.

You can also edit screenshots with the Photos app. You can crop, rotate and add text to the screenshot to make it more interesting.

Finally, if you want to delete a screenshot, simply open it in the Photos app and tap the “Trash” icon. The screenshot will be deleted permanently.

2. Different Methods of Taking Screenshots on iPhone 12#

  1. You can take a screenshot on your iPhone 12 using a combination of the Volume Up and Power buttons. Press them simultaneously and your device will take a screenshot.
  2. If you have an iPhone with a Home button as well, you can press the Home button and the Power button or the Home button and the Volume Up button to take a screenshot.

  5. If you have an Apple Pencil, you can take a screenshot by tapping the pencil on the screen while pressing and holding the Volume Up button.
  6. You can also use the Apple Watch to take a screenshot by using the Digital Crown and Side button simultaneously.

3. Accessing Screenshots on iPhone 12#

To begin, look at the right side of your iPhone 12. At the top you should see two buttons: The Volume Up button, and the Side Button (also known as the Power Button). Now, simultaneously press and release both the Volume Up and Power buttons. You will hear a camera shutter sound, and that signifies that a screenshot has been taken.

The screenshot will then take a few moments to appear on your display. You should then see a thumbnail of the screenshot in the bottom left corner of your display which, when tapped, will lead you to the screenshot. From here, you can view it, edit it, or share it with others.

An alternative method to take screenshots is to activate Siri. To do this, press and hold the Side button for a few seconds until the Siri prompt appears. Then utter the phrase ‘take a screenshot’ and Siri will take a screenshot for you.

If you wish to save the screenshot, you can do it by pressing the quality setting icon in the top right corner of the screenshot preview page. This will then give you the option to save the image to either Photos or Files.

If you want to quickly find a recently taken screenshot, swiping left from the Home screen or dragging down the notification shade on the Lock screen will display all the screenshots you have taken in the past.

Once you have taken a screenshot, you can also share it, either through Messages, AirDrop, or other apps. To share it, simply tap the share icon in the bottom left corner of the image.

You now know how to take screenshots on your iPhone 12. It’s easy, just remember to press and hold the Volume Up & Power buttons simultaneously to capture!

4. Editing and Sharing Screenshots on iPhone 12#

Once you have located the right buttons, you can easily capture a screenshot of anything that is displayed on your device. Press and hold the Volume Up button and the Power/Side button at the same time for a few seconds. You should then hear a shutter sound, and a screenshot of your display should be saved to your device.

To view your screenshot, open the Photos app, and tap on the Albums tab. Scroll down and tap on “Screenshot” to find all of your images. Additionally, you can also view your screenshots directly from the “Recents” tab located along the bottom of the Photos app.

To edit or share your screenshot, tap on the image in the Photos app. This will open the image in full-screen mode. At the bottom of the image, you will see an Edit and Share button. Tap on the Share button to easily share the screenshot to other applications.

Editing tools are also available if you want to enhance your screenshot. Tap on the Edit button to open the Image Editor. This full-featured editor lets you adjust color, contrast, sharpen, and more. When you are finished editing your image, make sure to tap on Done. You can also tap on the Revert button at any time to go back to the original image.

Your screenshot will survive reboots of your iPhone 12, so you can revisit your favorite screenshots whenever you want. If you want to delete a screenshot, just select it in the Photos app and tap on the Trash can icon to delete it. With this guide, you should now be able to successfully capture, edit, and share screenshots using your iPhone 12.
