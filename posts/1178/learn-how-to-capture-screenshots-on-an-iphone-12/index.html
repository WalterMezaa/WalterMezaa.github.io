Guide Capture Screenshots iPhone 12 Objective: In this tutorial, you will learn how to take screenshots on an iPhone 12.

Step 1. On your iPhone 12, press the Side Button and the Volume Up button at the same time.

Step 2. The screen will flash white and you will hear a sound indicating that a screenshot has been taken.

Step 3. Open the Photos app on your iPhone 12 and you will find the screenshot in the Recents tab.

Step 4. You can also find the screenshot by going to the Albums tab and clicking on the Screenshots album.

Step 5. To edit the screenshot, tap the image and select the edit button. Here you can crop, rotate, and add annotations.

Step 6. When you’re finished editing, tap Done and then tap Save to save the changes to your screenshot.

Congratulations! You have learned how to take and edit screenshots on an iPhone 12.

2. Exploring the Steps to Capture Screenshots on an iPhone 12#

  1. When you need to capture something that you see on your screen, you can take a screenshot on your iPhone 12.
  2. Capturing a screenshot is pretty simple: just press and hold the Side button and the Volume Up button simultaneously.
  3. When both buttons are held down, the screen will flash and a sound will play, indicating that the screenshot has been taken.
  4. The screenshot will be saved in your Photos app, in the Recents tab.
  5. In the Photos app, you can view, edit, or even delete the screenshot.
  6. To share the screenshot, you can select it and tap the Share button. From there, you will be able to send the image to another person or share it through other apps such as Facebook, Twitter, and more.
