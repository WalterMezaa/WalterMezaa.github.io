A Beginner s Guide to Taking Screenshots on iPhone 12 Step 1: Locate the Sleep/Wake And Volume Buttons On your iPhone 12, locate the side button and the volume buttons. The sleep/wake button is on the right side of the device, while the volume buttons are on the left side.

Step 2: Press the Sleep/Wake and Volume Up Buttons Simultaneously Once you have located the buttons, press and hold the Sleep/Wake button and the Volume Up button at the same time. Don’t release the buttons until you see the screenshot animation.

Step 3: View Your Screenshot The screenshot is saved automatically in your Photos app. To view it, simply open the Photos app, scroll down and select “Screenshots” to view all of your screenshots.

Step 4: Edit and Share Your Screenshot Once you’ve viewed the screenshot, you can make any edits you’d like. You can crop it, add text, draw on it, or add a filter. You can also share your screenshot directly to any social media platform or messaging app by tapping the share button.

1. Overview of Print Screen on iPhone 12.#

First, identify the Sleep/Wake button on your iPhone 12, it is located on the right side of the device. Then, press and hold down the Sleep/Wake button for at least two seconds. While still holding down the Sleep/Wake button, press and hold the Volume Up button located on the left side of the device. You will now notice the screen has been slighty dimmed and a camera sound will be played, indicating a screenshot has been taken.

After the screenshot has been taken, it will automatically be saved to your Photos application. To find the screenshot, open the Photos app and locate the most recent photo, this will be your screenshot. You can now further edit the screenshot by tapping the Edit button located in the top right corner of the application.

You may also add your screenshot to any other applications such as WhatsApp, Instagram or other instant messaging services. To do this, tap the Share button located in the bottom left corner of the Photos app when viewing the screenshot and select the application you would like to use to share.

Keep in mind that the screenshot will be stored as a JPEG file, meaning you are unable to keep an editable photo of your screenshot after you have further edited it. Additionally, you can also have a screenshot directly from the Photos app by tapping the screenshot and selecting the Share icon in the bottom left corner.

Also, you can view a history of your screenshots, this can be reached by tapping the Albums tab located at the bottom of the Photos app. Then select the Screenshots album and view all of your screenshots taken from your device.

Lastly, if you need to delete a screenshot, simply open the Photo app, select the screenshot you would like to delete and press the Trash icon located in the bottom right corner. Once deleted, the screenshot will no longer be visible in the Photos app.

2. How to Take a Print Screen on iPhone 12.#

  1. First, make sure that you have the two buttons pressed in the same time. The Sleep/Wake button is located on the right side of the iPhone 12 and the Volume Up button is located on the left side.
  2. You will hear a shutter sound along with a short animation, and you will find that your display has been captured as an image on the Photos app.
  3. If you find that your print screen did not turn out exactly as you wanted, you can edit the image on your Photos app. This can be done by tapping the image to show the editing toolbar.
  4. In the editing toolbar, you can crop your image, add effects, and more to make it perfect.
  5. Once you are done, you can save the edited image into your Photos app or share it with others.
  6. Finally, you can also take a screenshot on your iPhone 12 by using the AssistiveTouch feature. To enable this, open Settings > General > Accessibility and toggle on AssistiveTouch. Then, tap the AssistiveTouch icon and select the Device option. There, you will find the Screenshot option.
