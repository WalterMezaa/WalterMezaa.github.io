1. Overview of the tools needed to capture Android screen#

  1. To begin with, you need to enable USB debugging on your Android device. To do this, you need to go to ‘Settings’ on your home screen and then open the ‘System’ tab. From there, you need to open the ‘developer options’ tab and turn on the ‘USB Debugging’ switch.
  2. After activating USB Debugging, you will need to download the right recording software for your PC. Depending on your OS, you could use software like ApowerREC or Mobizen for Windows and ApowerMirror or Xrecorder for Mac.
  3. Once the software has been downloaded and installed, launch it and connect your Android device to your PC using a USB cable.
  4. The next step will be to select the area of the screen you want to record. On the software, you should be able to see the option to select the area to record.
  5. Once the area to record has been chosen, the audio settings should be adjusted according to your needs. On the software, there should be an option for the audio settings.
  6. Finally, you can begin recording the Android screen along with audio. To do this, click on the ‘Record’ button on the software. The recording will start immediately.

2. How to Connect Your Android Device to a PC#

  1. Firstly, you will need to enable USB Debugging Mode. To do so, go to Settings > About Phone > Tap the Build Number seven times. After that, go back to Settings > Developer Options > Enable USB Debugging Mode.
  2. Secondly, download the proper recording software for your device. Depending on the software, you may need to install drivers to your computer.
  3. Now, connect your Android device to the computer via USB Cable. Make sure that it is securely connected.
  4. Next, you can launch the recording software. You can configure the settings here, such as the video resolution, frame rate, and audio source.
  5. Then, you can select the area you want to record. After that, you can click on the “Record” button.
  6. Once finished, you can click the “Stop” button to stop the recording. The recording will be automatically saved in your chosen directory.

4. How to Record Android Screen on PC with Audio#

  1. Install the appropriate USB driver for your Android device on your PC. This will allow the two devices to connect, and the action of recording the Android device’s screen will start.
  2. Enable USB Debugging mode on your Android device. This will allow your device to be controlled from your PC.
  3. Download the ideal Android screen recording software on your PC. This will provide the necessary tools for you to capture a video of your Android’s screen.
  4. Plug-in your Android device to your PC using a USB cable. Launch the desired screen recorder and press the record button.
  5. Select the right settings for the video and audio recording as per your requirements.
  6. Once the recording is complete, unplug your Android device from the PC. You can either transfer the video file to your Android device for playback or save it on your PC.
