1. Introduction of Professional Quality Recording#

Professional quality recording is the process of capturing audio and video recordings with the highest clarity, such as what is needed in TV production, films and other related media. With the use of the right tools and equipment, it is possible to achieve professional quality recordings of your Android screen on PC with audio.

To begin, you need to connect your Android device to your PC either using a USB or WiFi connection. After that, you will have to launch the ApowerMirror app on both devices following the instructions given on the screen. Once the connection is established, you can open the Android app you wish to record and click the video recorder icon on your PC. To add audio, click the microphone icon on the video recorder bar.

To ensure that you get the highest quality audio, you may also opt to use an external microphone that is connected to your PC. Next, you can adjust the resolution, frames per second (fps) rate, bitrate, and audio settings. It is recommended to set the resolution to 720p, fps rate to 30, bitrate to 7000kbps, and audio settings to Auto select.

Once the audio and video settings are adjusted accordingly, you will be able to start recording your Android screen on PC with audio. After you stop recording, you can simply transfer the recorded video from your Android to your PC to save, edit or share.

2. Preparation for Recording Android Screen to PC with Audio#

  1. To begin, make sure that you have the latest version of your Android OS installed on your device.
  2. Install the ApowerMirror app on both your Android device and PC.
  3. Once ApowerMirror is installed on both devices, connect your Android device to the PC with either a USB cable or via WiFi.
  4. Launch ApowerMirror on both your devices and follow the onscreen instructions to successfully connect them.
  5. Once connected, open the Android app that you want to record on the PC and select the Video Recorder icon.
  6. On the PC, you then have the option to add audio to the recording by clicking on the microphone icon and choosing the audio device that you wish to use.

3. Recording Your Android Screen to PC with Audio#

  1. After clicking the Video Recorder icon on the PC, the mirror of the Android device will appear on the PC.
  2. Make sure that the settings are adjusted to display 1080p resolution and click the microphone icon to record the audio from the Android device.
  3. You can also adjust the volume as needed. If you only want to record the audio from the Android device, you can mute the PC audio.
  4. Once the settings have been adjusted to the preferences, click the record button to begin recording.
  5. After the recording is finished, the recorded video will be automatically saved to the PC.
  6. You can now review the video to check if the quality is up to professional standards.

4. Exporting and Sharing the Recording#

After the recording, you will be asked if you want to save the video or not. Then the video will be exported, and you can share your recording directly to YouTube, Facebook, and other social media platforms. You can also save it to your computer. This is the most convenient way to share your recordings if you want to show it to the world. In addition, you can save it as an MP4 or GIF format or upload it to Google Drive, Dropbox, and other cloud storage platforms.
