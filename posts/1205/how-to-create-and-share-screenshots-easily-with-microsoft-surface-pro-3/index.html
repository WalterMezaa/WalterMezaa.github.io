Step by Step Guide on How to Take a Screenshot on Microsoft Surface Pro 3 Creating a Screenshot on Microsoft Surface Pro 3

  1. On your Surface Pro 3 desktop, open the app or program window which you want to screenshot.
  2. Press the Windows button and the Volume Down button simultaneously.
  3. To view the screenshot, open your Pictures folder and check the Screenshots folder.

Sharing Your Screenshot from Microsoft Surface Pro 3

  1. Open the Screenshots folder in the Pictures folder and locate the screenshot you want to share.
  2. Right-click the screenshot and select Share.
  3. Select the app or program you want to share the screenshot with.
  4. Enter the name or email address of the person you want to share the screenshot with.
  5. Enter an optional message with the screenshot and click Send.

  1. Before taking a screenshot, check to make sure your device has enough space to store the image.
  2. If you want to modify the screenshots before sharing, you can also use a photo editing software such as Adobe Photoshop.
  3. Make sure that the Volume Down function is enabled on your device.
  4. To take a screenshot, press and hold the Windows and Volume Down buttons at the same time.
  5. You can confirm that the screenshot was taken successfully by looking in Pictures > Screenshots and check if the image is now visible.
  6. If you would like to share the screenshot, open the Screenshots folder and select the image you’d like to share.

II. Capturing Screenshots#

  1. To edit the screenshot before sharing, tap the Draw tool on the screenshot, and then tap Markup or Draw.
  2. After making your edits, select Done > Save.
  3. To quickly share the edited screenshot, select Share > Select the appropriate apps you would like to share the screenshot to.
  4. If you choose, you can copy the screenshot to the clipboard by selecting Copy.
  5. If you want to save and share the screenshot to a cloud storage service, you can tap the plus sign at the bottom right of the screen and select the appropriate service.
  6. Last but not least, if you don’t need to edit the screenshot you can simply select Share > Pick an appropriate app you want to share with.
