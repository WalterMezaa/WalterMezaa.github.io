How to Take Screenshots on an ASUS Chromebook

  1. Open the window or application you want to capture.
  2. Press the “Ctrl + Show Windows” keys simultaneously. If the “Show Windows” is not part of the keyboard, press “Ctrl + F5” instead.
  3. A screenshot of the window will be automatically saved.
  4. To access the screenshot, open the Files app and select “Downloads”.
  5. Your screenshot should be saved here.
  6. If you want to take a screenshot of only part of the window, press the “Ctrl + Shift + Show Windows” keys simultaneously.
  7. A gray rectangle icon will appear on your screen. Drag it to the required area and click on it to take the screenshot.
  8. To end the process, press the “Ctrl + Show Windows” keys again.
  9. The screenshot of the selected area will automatically be saved.
  10. To access the screenshot, open the Files app and select “Downloads”. Your screenshot should be saved here.

1. Introduction to Taking Screenshots on Your ASUS Chromebook#

You can use either of the shortcuts mentioned above to take a screenshot on your ASUS Chromebook. The screenshot will be automatically saved to the Files > Downloads folder. This makes it quite simple to access the screenshot directly from the Downloads folder. To capture a specific window, simply open the window before pressing the keys. The captured screenshot will be the exact same window when the shortcut keys are pressed. Furthermore, you can also take partial screenshots on your ASUS Chromebook. Simply press the Shift+Ctrl+Show Windows keys followed by the left mouse button to select the area you would like to capture. The partial screenshot will be saved in the same Downloads folder as the full screenshot.

2. Different Capturing Methods on ASUS Chromebook#

You can also capture a specific portion of the screen by pressing Shift+Ctrl+Show Windows or Shift+Ctrl+F5 keys. The cursor will be changed to the cross-hair when you press on those keys. Then, you can move your cursor and select the area to be captured before releasing the mouse button. The captured screenshot will be stored in the Downloads directory.

Moreover, you can capture a scrolling screenshot of a window or page by pressing Ctrl+Shift+Show Windows or Ctrl+Shift+F5. Once you press the keys, a guide bar will appear at the top of the window. You can click and drag the guide bar to the bottom of the page to capture the entire screenshot. This screenshot will be also stored in the Downloads directory.

ASUS Chromebook also offers the option of creating an annotation of the screenshot with the help of the built-in image editor. You can access the image editor by right-clicking on the screenshot in the Downloads folder. You can add arrows, text, boxes, and stamps to the screenshot as you like.

Furthermore, you can share the screenshot with others quickly. The screenshot can be shared via other apps, such as Cloud storage, Google Drive, and online messaging apps. To share the screenshot, simply right-click on its thumbnail in the Downloads folder and select the app from where you want to share the screenshot.

Lastly, you can save the screenshot to the clipboard by pressing Shift+Ctrl+C keys. You can directly paste the screenshot from the clipboard to any other app. The screenshot will be removed from the clipboard automatically after some time.

By following these simple steps you can easily capture screenshots and capture specific portions of the screen as well as a scrolling screenshot of a window or page on your ASUS Chromebook.

3. Keyboard Shortcuts Overview#

You can also use the Ctrl+Shift+Show Windows or Ctrl+Shift+F5 keyboard shortcut to take a screenshot of a specific area of the screen. To take a screenshot of a specific window, open it before pressing the keys. If you want to take a screenshot of the entire screen, press Ctrl+F5 or Ctrl+View keys. The image will be saved in the Chromebook’s Downloads folder. Additionally, after pressing the keys, an animation will appear at the corner of the screen to confirm that the screenshot has been captured.

4. How to Save and Share Your Screenshots on ASUS Chromebook#

After capturing a screenshot, the image can be found in Files > Downloads. From there, you can rename, edit, and share it with others. To rename the image, simply right-click on the file and select ‘Rename’. You can additionally preview the photo by double-clicking the file.

To edit the screenshot, you can download and install the Chrome Web Store app ‘Pixlr’. This app allows you to adjust the colors and brightness of screenshots as well as add text, shapes, and other decorations.

To share the screenshot, simply right-click on the image to open up a contextual menu which includes the ‘Share’ option. You can choose to send the image via email, SMS, or other supported apps. You can also post the image directly to various social media sites such as Facebook and Twitter.

When you’re finished editing and sharing, you can delete the screenshot from the Downloads folder. This will ensure the image doesn’t take up unnecessary storage space on your device.

Q: How do I capture a screenshot on my ASUS Chromebook? A: To take a screenshot of your ASUS Chromebook, press the “Ctrl” key and the “Switch window” key simultaneously. This will save the image of your screen to your local Downloads folder.

Q: What key combination do I use to take a partial screenshot on my ASUS Chromebook? A: To take a partial screenshot on your ASUS Chromebook, press and hold the “Ctrl” and “Shift” keys, then press the “Switch window” key. This will open a small selection window that you can drag around your screen to capture the desired area.

Q: Is it possible to save my screenshots in a different folder on my ASUS Chromebook? A: Absolutely. To change where your screenshots are saved, open the “Settings” app and go to the “Device” section. From here, you can choose to save screenshots to a different folder or even an external storage device.

Q: Can I take screenshots of my ASUS Chromebook’s screen while it is off? A: No, currently it is not possible to take screenshots of the ASUS Chromebook screen while it is off.

Q: How do I use the screenshots I take on my ASUS Chromebook? A: You can use screenshots taken with your ASUS Chromebook to share with others, upload to social media, or save for future reference. To view your screenshots, open the “Files” app and select the “Downloads” folder. From here you can open and edit the captured images.
