Guide Screenshot Asus Chromebook Taking screenshots on an ASUS Chromebook is easy. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Press and hold the ‘Ctrl’ and ‘Window Switcher’ buttons at the same time. The ‘Window Switcher’ button can be found in between the ‘Alt’ and ‘Brightness’ keys.

  2. You should now see a notification that a screenshot was taken. The screenshot will be saved in the ‘Downloads’ folder.

  3. If you want to take a screenshot of the whole screen, press the ‘Ctrl’ and ‘Full Screen’ buttons at the same time. The ‘Full Screen’ button is located in the top row of the keyboard.

  4. To take a partial screenshot, press the ‘Ctrl’ and ‘Shift’ buttons at the same time. Then, select an area of the screen to capture.
