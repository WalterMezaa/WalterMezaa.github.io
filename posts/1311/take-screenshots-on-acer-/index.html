1. Overview on Taking Screenshots on Acer Windows 10 Laptop#

Taking screenshots on a laptop equipped with Windows 10 is much easier than you think. All you have to do is press two buttons simultaneously on your Acer laptop to take a picture of the display. First, you have to locate your Windows logo key. It’s usually located near the lower left side of the keyboard. Then, you have to press the Windows logo key and the Print Screen button on the keyboard at the same time. After that, the screenshot will be saved in the “Screenshots” folder which is located in the “Pictures” library. To open the folder, you can simply right click on the “Pictures” library and select “Open”. Once you have opened the folder, you can select the image you need for your project. You can also use the screenshot to share with your friends or post it on social media. Additionally, you can also upload the screenshot to an online storage site for future use.

2. Step-by-Step Guide on Taking a Screenshot#

  1. To take a screenshot on your Acer Windows 10 laptop, simply press and hold the Windows Logo key and the PrtScr button (Print Screen) from your keyboard at the same time.
  2. After pressing both the keys together, a screenshot will be taken and stored in the Screenshots folder within the Pictures library.
  3. To access the screenshots, open the Pictures library and click on the Screenshots folder to see the screenshot taken.
  4. The screenshot file saved will be a .png file by default.
  5. You can also capture a screenshot of the entire display by using the Windows Logo key plus the Shift key and the PrtScr button simultaneously.
  6. The screenshot can then be edited using Paint or any other image editing program, just like any other image. You can also share it via email or upload it to a cloud storage.

3. How to Take Partial Screenshots#

Taking a partial screenshot on Acer Windows 10 laptops is quite simple. To do this, press the Windows Logo Key + Shift Key + S together. Doing this will gray out the screen and the cursor will change to a plus symbol. Using the plus symbol, draw a box around the area you want to capture and release the mouse. Your partial screenshot will be saved to the clipboard. Paste this screenshot into a document or image editor to save it.

You can also take an entire screenshot of your laptop by pressing the Windows Logo Key + PrtScr together. Upon doing this, the screenshot file will be saved to Screenshot folder in your Pictures library. You can open this folder to locate the screenshot. Once you have located the image, click on it to select it and press Ctrl + S to save the file.

If you need to save a screenshot as an HTML file, press the Windows Logo Key + Alt + PrtScr together. On doing so, the page will be saved as an HTML file on your desktop. You can then open the file in your browser to view it.

If you want to capture an active window, press the Alt + PrtScr keys at the same time. This will capture the window you are currently working in and it will automatically be copied to the clipboard. Again you can paste it into a document or image editor to save it.

To take a screenshot of a specific app window, simultaneously press the Windows Logo Key + Shift Key + S. Doing this will open a menu at the top of the window and you can easily select the window you want to capture. The screenshot will be saved in your Pictures library. Open the folder to view and save the image.

4. Summary and Tips on Taking Screenshots#

The process of taking a screenshot on an Acer Windows 10 laptop is easy. After pressing the Windows Logo key and PrtScr together, the screenshot will be saved in the Screenshot folder in Pictures library. Once the picture has been saved in the folder, you can open the folder and select the image.

Apart from taking screenshots with Windows Logo key and PrtScr, you can also use the Snipping Tool, which is available on Windows 10. This tool allows you to capture a specific area of the screen. You can also delay the screenshot capture by some seconds. If you don’t want to take a screenshot of the entire screen, then the Snipping Tool is the best option.

If you want to take screenshots of games or apps, you can use the Game Bar. This feature allows you to take screenshots of your game play. It also allows you to record your game play if you want to show others how to complete a level or show off a particularly difficult move.

In addition to taking screenshots, you can also take a recording of your desktop. This recording will capture your entire desktop and you can then save the file for later reference. This can be useful for tutorials or help videos that you may need to record for others.

Finally, you can also use the clipboard to take a screenshot. To do this, simply press CTRL+V and the screenshot will be stored in your clipboard. This can be useful if you want to quickly copy a certain part of a screenshot to another application.

In summary, taking screenshots on your Acer Windows 10 laptop is easy. You can use the Windows Logo key and PrtScr, the Snipping Tool, the Game Bar and the clipboard to take screenshots on your laptop. All of these methods are easy to use and can be helpful in different situations.
