Step by Step Guide to Screenshot on an Acer Laptop with Windows 10 If you own an Acer laptop running Windows 10, you can easily take screenshots using the built-in screenshot tool. To do so, press the Windows key and PrtScn key on your keyboard at the same time. The screenshot will be saved in your Pictures folder. You can also access the screenshot tool from the Power menu in the Start Menu. If the screenshot tool is already open, you can just press the PrtScn button to take a screenshot. 1. Overview of Screenshot Tool on Windows 10#

Taking screenshots of your Acer laptop running Windows 10 has been made much simpler with the built-in screenshot tool. The most popular and easiest way to take screenshots is to press the Windows key and the PrtScn key together simultaneously. This will capture the entire screen of the device. Saved screenshots can be easily accessed via the Pictures folder in the File Explorer. The screenshot tool can also be accessed from the Power menu of the Start Menu, or can be opened directly by pressing the PrtScn key if the screenshot tool is already open. Using the screenshot tool, you can also take partial screenshots of the device. In addition, you can annotate and crop images and even share them directly to social media sites.

2. How to Take a Screenshot on Windows 10#

Taking a screenshot of your Acer laptop running Windows 10 is easy thanks to its built-in screenshot taking tool. All you need to do is press the Windows and PrtScn keys simultaneously on your keyboard, and a screenshot of your screen will be captured. You can find the saved screenshots in your Pictures folder. As an alternative, you can access the screenshot tool through the Power menu from the Start Menu or press PrtScn if the tool has been open already. You can also take screenshots with the Snipping Tool which lets you select an area of the screen to be saved. Lastly, pressing Alt and PrtScn at the same time will take screenshots of the active window. With these easy steps you will be able to capture whatever you need and keep memories and information safe in your device.

3. Adjusting Screenshot Settings on an Acer Laptop#

To take a screenshot with the tool, open the tool with the Win + PrtScn shortcut and select the area you want to capture. If no area is specified, the entire screen will be captured. The screenshot will then be saved to the Pictures folder. You can edit the screenshot and even share it by selecting the Share option in the upper right corner of the Screen Sketch app.

To customize the screenshot settings, go to Settings > Ease of Access > Keyboard > Print Screen Shortcut. Here you can adjust the shortcut function to take a screenshot and open the tool, just take a screenshot, or open the tool without taking a screenshot.

Additionally, you can enable the delay function to capture menus and pop-ups. This can be done by going to Settings > Ease of Access > Keyboard > Print Screen Shortcut. There, you can enable the delay feature and set the amount of time you’d like the screen to wait before a screenshot is taken.

You can also customize the default destination for screenshots by going to Settings > System > Storage. Here, you’ll be able to select which folder you’d like the screenshots to be saved in. Lastly, you can choose the file type of the screenshots, either JPEG, PNG, or GIF.

4. Tips to Take the Best Screenshots on an Acer Laptop#

Apart from pressing the Windows key and PrtScn simultaneously, there are other ways to use the screenshot tool on an Acer laptop. You can access the tool via the Start Menu, and then choose the Power icon. You may also open the tool manually and then hit the PrtScn key whenever you want to take a shot. All screenshots you take will be saved into the Pictures folder. To locate it on your laptop, select the File Explorer icon on the taskbar. Then, double-click on This PC and go into your Pictures folder. Once you’re there, all of the screenshots you’ve taken should be visible. If not, try clicking on the Date Modified tab to make them easier to find.
