2. Steps to take a screenshot on a Lenovo IdeaPad laptop#

  1. Open your Start Menu, and search for the Snipping Tool in the search bar. The snipping tool is an application that is already installed on your Lenovo IdeaPad, it works by capturing all or part of your laptop’s screen.
  2. Click on New and select the area of the screen you would like to capture. Once you have clicked on the New button, the area you have chosen will be highlighted.
  3. Now select the type of snipping you’d like to create. You can choose from options including free-form, rectangular, window, or full-screen.
  4. You may draw on the snipped image using the drawing tools found on the toolbar to the left. This can be useful for adding arrows, highlighting a specific area, or making other annotations.
  5. Once you are happy with the snipped image you have created, click Save As to save the image to a location of your choice.
  6. You may also send the image via email, attach it to a document or simply copy the image to the clipboard. You can then paste the image in an application of your choice.

3. Taking screenshots for different versions of Windows#

For Windows 7 versions, the Snipping Tool can be opened by clicking the Start button, then type “Snipping Tool” in the search box in the taskbar. The Snipping Tool application will be displayed in the search results. Click on the application to open it. Once you have opened the application you’ll be able to capture a region, window, full screen or you can also use touch screen devices to draw with your finger or stylus.

For Windows 8 versions or later, you can use the Mod + Prt Scrn to take a screenshot. To open the Prt SCrn function, press Window key and Prt Scrn altogether. The screenshot will be saved in a file in PNG format. You can also press Window key + Shift + S then select the area you want to capture.

For Windows 10 and later versions, you can use the same PrtScrn technique mentioned above or use the Snipping Tool. The Snipping Tool application is available in the Start Menu. Open the Snipping Tool and use the same function as described for Windows 7. You can also press Window key + Shift + S then draw over the area you want to capture.

Q.1 How can I take a screenshot on my Lenovo IdeaPad laptop? A. You can take a screenshot on your Lenovo IdeaPad laptop easily by pressing the “Print Screen” (PrtScn) key on your keyboard. This key can be found in the top row of your keyboard. Alternatively, you can press “Alt” + “Print Screen” keys simultaneously.

Q.2 How can I save the screenshot I took on my Lenovo IdeaPad laptop? A. To save the screenshot you took on your Lenovo IdeaPad laptop, open the Windows Paint application and press “Ctrl” + “V” at the same time. This will paste the screenshot onto the Paint application where you can choose to save it as an image file.

Q.3 How do I take a screenshot of the whole screen on my Lenovo IdeaPad laptop? A. To take a screenshot of the whole screen on your Lenovo IdeaPad laptop, press the “Windows” key + “Print Screen” key simultaneously. This will save the image of the whole screen into your Pictures folder where you can access it and save it as an image file.

Q.4 How do I take a screenshot of only a certain part of my screen on my Lenovo IdeaPad laptop? A. To take a screenshot of only a certain part of your screen, press the “Windows” key + “Shift” + “S” keys simultaneously. This will bring up the “Snip & Sketch” tool, where you can select the area of the screen you want to take a screenshot of. Once you have selected the area, click the “Save” button to save it as an image file.

Q.5 How do I take a screenshot of a single window on my Lenovo IdeaPad laptop? A. To take a screenshot of a single window on your Lenovo IdeaPad laptop, press the “Alt” + “Print Screen” keys simultaneously. This will take a screenshot of the active window only and save it to your clipboard. You can then paste it onto a Paint application and save it as an image file.
