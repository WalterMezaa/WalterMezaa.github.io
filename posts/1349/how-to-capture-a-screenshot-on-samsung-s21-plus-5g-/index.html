Q4. How do I edit screenshots on a Samsung S21 Plus 5G? A4. You can easily edit screenshots on your Samsung S21 Plus 5G. Open the screenshot in the Gallery app, then tap the Edit icon at the bottom of the screen. From there, you can crop and rotate the image, or adjust brightness, contrast, and more.

Q5. How do I share screenshots on a Samsung S21 Plus 5G? A5. You can easily share screenshots from your Samsung S21 Plus 5G.
