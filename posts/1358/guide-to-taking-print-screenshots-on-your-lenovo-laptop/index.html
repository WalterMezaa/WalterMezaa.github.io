Step 1: Open the window or image you want to capture. You will be saving a copy of this open window or image to print.

Step 2: Press the ‘Ctrl + PrtScn’ keys simultaneously on your keyboard. This is the same combination of keys for many Windows computers. For some laptops, you may need to press the ‘Fn’ key first, followed by the ‘Ctrl’ and ‘PrtScn’ keys. This will save a screenshot of your current window.

Step 3: To save this screenshot, open the Image Editing software of your choice and create a new document. It is a good practice to make sure the size of the document matches the size of the window you captured.

Step 4: To paste the screenshot onto the new document, press the ‘Ctrl + V’ keys simultaneously. This is the same combination of keys for many Windows computers. Alternatively, you can right-click the document and select ‘Paste’.

Step 5: Save your screenshot by clicking ‘File’ and then ‘Save As’. You can choose the format and desired location of the file before saving it.

Step 6: Your screenshot is now saved and ready to be printed. Open the file from the desired location and click the ‘Print’ button. Select the printer you want to use to print the screenshot.
