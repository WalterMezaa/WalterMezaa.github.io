1. Introduction to Taking Screen Shots on a Lenovo laptop#

Taking screenshots in a Lenovo laptop is easy. The first step is to select the window or area that you want to capture. Then, press the “Print Screen” button (usually located at the top of the keyboard, and may say “Prt Sc”, “PrtScn”, “Prnt Scrn”). Once the screenshot is taken, open an image editing program such as Microsoft Paint or Adobe to edit the image. You can decide to save the file in different formats such as PNG, JPG, or GIF. It is also possible to crop or rotate the image, add text, or draw on the image. After that, you can save the image, share it via social media, or set it as a wallpaper.

2. How to Take a Screen Shot on a Lenovo Laptop#

Lenovo laptops have an adaptable design which allows users to take a screenshot of a chosen window or area. First select the window or area to capture, then pressing the Print Screen button which is typically located at the top of the keyboard. It may be labeled as Prt Sc, PrtScn, or Prnt Scrn. After pressing the Print Screen key, open an image editing program such as Microsoft Paint or Adobe to paste the image into the program. 3. What to Do After Taking a Screen Shot of Your Lenovo Laptop#

Once the Print Screen button is pressed the screenshot will be saved to your clipboard. To access the image, open an image editing program such as Microsoft Paint or Adobe Photoshop. 4. Tips for Capturing a Quality Screen Shot on Your Lenovo Laptop#

When using Windows 10, you have the added ability to use the Snipping Tool to capture a screenshot. This tool allows you to draw any shape you want around the area you want to capture and you can even have a 5-second delay for taking the screenshot. You can access the “Snipping Tool” via the Windows search box, then you can save it as an image file.

Furthermore, you can also use the Game Bar in Windows 10 to capture a screenshot of the game you are currently playing. To do this, press the “Windows Key”+”G”, and when the Game Bar appears, press the camera icon. The captured screenshot will be saved to your computer’s Pictures folder.

If those methods don’t work for you, then you can utilize the “Lenovo OneKey Recovery”. This allows you to create a disk image of the current state of your laptop, which also includes taking screenshots. To use, press the “Novo” button on your laptop and then select the “OneKey Recovery” option.

In addition, you can also use screenshot software like Snagit (a product from TechSmith) to capture a screenshot of your Lenovo laptop. This software allows you to capture a screenshot of a specific area or all of the screen and then save the image in any supported format.

Finally, you can also use the Windows key + H keyboard shortcut to capture a screenshot of your Lenovo laptop. This will open the “Share Charm” and from there you can share the screenshot with your social media or send it via email. It will be automatically saved in your Pictures folder.
