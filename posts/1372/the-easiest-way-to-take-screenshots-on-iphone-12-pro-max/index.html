You will then hear the familiar camera shutter sound indicating that you have taken a screenshot. You can then find your screenshots in the Photos app. If you need to share the screenshot, you can do so quickly and easily from within the Photos app.

You can also use Siri to take a screenshot. Just activate Siri and say “take a screenshot”. This will take a screenshot of whatever is on the display.

For more advanced options, you can press and hold the side button and volume up button for a few seconds. This will open the screenshot options menu. Here, you can choose to crop, highlight, or draw on your screenshot before saving it.

2. Steps to Take a Screenshot#

Taking a screenshot on the iPhone 12 Pro Max is a simple process. To capture the screen, all you need to do is press and hold the Side button and the Volume Up button at the same time. Once you press both buttons, you will hear a camera shutter sound, confirming that the screenshot has been taken. The screenshot can then be found and shared from the Photos app. Additionally, you can use AssistiveTouch to take a screenshot of your screen instead of using the side and volume up buttons. To do this, navigate to Settings, then Accessibility, and toggle AssistiveTouch On. Selecting Device, followed by More, and then Screenshot will allow you to take a screenshot easily.

3. How to Edit a Screenshot#

Once you take a screenshot, you can edit it with the Preview window for that screenshot. You can tap the markup icon at the top left of the window to add texts, squares, or highlights. To turn a highlight into a drawing, you can tap the highlight button at the bottom and then use the drawing tools on the left. You can also add your signature or magnify certain parts of the screenshot. When you’re done, tap ‘Done’ to save your changes.
