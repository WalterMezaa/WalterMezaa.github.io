1. Introduction to Split Screen Feature on Android 13#

Split Screen is a feature on Android 13 that allows you to view two different apps side-by-side in the same window. This feature is useful for multitasking, watching videos and working on two apps simultaneously. It’s easy to access the Split Screen feature on Android 13, and the steps you need to take are listed below.

Firstly, go to your Settings and activate Force activities to be resizable. Once activated the feature should be ready to use, so you can proceed to the next step.

Secondly, open any app you want. and press and hold the recent apps button. Scroll through the list of open apps and select the one you want to open in the split-screen mode.

Thirdly, long-press the app icon and drag it to the top or the bottom of the screen. Once you’ve placed the app, release the icon and the two apps will be split in half.

Fourthly, you can make adjustments to the screen split. To do this, press and hold the middle line between the two apps. Drag the line to resize the apps as you desire.

Finally, press the recent apps button again to exit the split-screen mode. Enjoy being able to view two different apps at the same time.

2. How to Activate the Split Screen Feature#

  1. To activate the split screen feature on Android 13, go to Settings and search for Force activities to be resizable.
  2. Once you’ve enabled it, open any app, and press and hold the recent apps button.
  3. This will show all the apps which were recently used, from which you can select the app you want to open.
  4. Both apps will now be open simultaneously. To adjust the screen sizes, drag and drop the blue line adjusting the size of the apps.
  5. You can swap the position of the apps by clicking the swap position button at the top of the blue line.
  6. Android 13 comes with an auto-fill feature which allows you to quickly switch between apps while using the split-screen mode. To enable this, select the enable auto-fill button.

3. What Types of Apps are Compatible with Split Screen#

Split screen mode allows you to use two apps simultaneously on your Android 13. To do this, you have to enable the force activities to be resizable feature on the settings. Not all apps are compatible with this feature, but some of the compatible types of apps are games, navigation, and video players. Some of the most popular apps used in split screen mode are Google Maps, YouTube, and Amazon Prime. To be able to enjoy this feature, make sure that both apps are compatible with split screen. You can also open the recent apps list and choose the apps you want to open. After that, you can resize each app and enjoy the split screen mode.

4. Tips & Tricks for Using the Split Screen Feature#

  1. To use this feature, the user must turn on the split-screen function in their Android device. This can be done in the settings menu.
  2. To use the split-screen feature, the user should first open any application from the list.
  3. After the app is open, the user needs to press and hold the recent apps button at the bottom of their device.
  4. Once the button is pressed, a split-screen activation icon will appear and the user can select the desired app from the list.
  5. After selecting an app, the user can now adjust the display ratio between the two open apps by dragging the bar.
  6. Finally, users can maximize the display of an app by tapping on the expand icon at the top of their display.

Q1: What is Split Screen on Android 13? A1: Split Screen on Android 13 is a feature that allows users to view two apps side by side on the same display. This is useful for multitasking and quickly switching between apps.

Q2: How do I access Split Screen on Android 13? A2: To access Split Screen on Android 13, you must first open the app you want to use in Split Screen mode. Then, press and hold the Recent Apps (the square icon) button and select the app you want to open in Split Screen mode.

Q3: How can I adjust the size of the Split Screen apps? A3: To adjust the size of the Split Screen apps, you can drag the divider bar that appears between the two apps. You can move the bar to the right to give the lower app more space, or to the left to give the upper app more space.

Q4: How do I close Split Screen on Android 13? A4: To close Split Screen on Android 13, press and hold the Recent Apps button, then select the X icon on the top right of the app window that you want to close. You can also drag the divider bar all the way to the bottom or top of the screen to close the app.

Q5: Can I use Split Screen on Android 13 with multiple apps? A5: Yes, you can use Split Screen on Android 13 with multiple apps. All you have to do is open one app in Split Screen mode, and then add additional apps by pressing and holding the Recent Apps button and selecting the app you want to open.
