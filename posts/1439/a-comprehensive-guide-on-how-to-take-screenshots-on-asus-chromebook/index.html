Guide to Screenshot ASUS Introduction Taking screenshots on your Asus Chromebook is a great way to capture important information, document your work, or share something with a friend. With a few simple steps, you can quickly take screenshots on your Chromebook and save them locally or in the cloud.

Steps

  1. First, open the window or tab that you want to take a screenshot of.
  2. Press the “Ctrl” and “Window Switch” keys at the same time. The “Window Switch” key will typically look like a box with two lines at the top.
  3. You will see a brief animation that indicates the screenshot has been taken. You will also hear a shutter sound.
  4. Your screenshot will be automatically saved into the “Downloads” folder of your Chromebook.
  5. If you want to save the screenshot to the cloud, click on the “Files” app icon.
  6. Select the screenshot you just took, and then click on the “Share” button (it looks like an arrow) in the upper-right corner of the window.
  7. Select the online service you want to save the screenshot to. You can choose from Google Drive, Google Photos, or any other compatible online service.
  8. Once you click on the service of your choice, follow the instructions to finish saving the screenshot in the cloud.
