Step by Step Guide on How to Take a Screenshot on a Lenovo Laptop Windows 10

Take Screenshot Lenovo Win10

Taking Screenshots Easily with Your Lenovo Laptop Windows 10 Taking screenshots quickly on your Lenovo laptop Windows 10 is easy. Here’s a comprehensive guide on how to do it.

  1. Press the ‘Windows key + Print Screen’. This shortcut takes an image of your entire screen and automatically saves it in the Screenshots folder of your Pictures directory.

  2. Open the ‘Snipping Tool’. This is a screen capturing utility which can be found in the ‘Start menu’ under ‘All Programs’. If needed you can type ‘Snipping Tool’ in the search box to locate it. This tool lets you capture a free-form area, rectangular area, active window or a full screen capture.

  3. Select the ‘Snipping Tool’ icon. This icon looks like a paper clip with a pair of scissors. Once you select it, the screen will freeze and the cursor will turn into a plus sign.

  4. Use the cursor to select the area of the screen that you wish to capture. Once you have the area selected, the selection will be highlighted and the image will be saved to the clipboard.

  5. Go to an image editing program such as Microsoft Paint and ‘Paste’ the captured image. You can now save the image to your hard drive.

This is how you take screenshots quickly on your Lenovo laptop Windows 10. It is simple and quick. Just remember to save your captured images to your hard drive so that you can access them later.

1. Introduction to Taking Screenshots on a Lenovo Windows 10 Laptop#

Taking a screenshot is simple and useful way of documenting and sharing information on your PC. For instance, if you want to share a screenshot of a document, an error message, what’s on your screen, etc. If you own a Lenovo laptop equipped with Windows 10, below are two useful methods to help you take screenshots quickly.

First, press the ‘Windows key + Print Screen’ button simultaneously to take a screenshot of the entire screen and automatically save it in the ‘Pictures/Screenshot folder’ in File Explorer.

You can also use the ‘Snipping Tool’ from the ‘Start menu’ to capture a selected portion of the screen. The Snipping Tool will open, allowing you to select the area of the screen you want to capture. It will then save it in the Pictures folder by default.

In addition, the Snipping Tool also gives you the option to use a timer, which can be useful for taking screenshots that require you to click specific buttons and menus.

Furthermore, the ‘Alt + Print Screen’ button combination is another way to capture an active window or desktop into the clipboard instead of saving it as a file. You can then paste the screenshot into Paint or Word, to add annotations, crop, or simply save the image.

If you want to capture a lengthy screen that cannot be displayed in a single screenshot, you can use the ‘Scrolling Capture’ feature. This feature will allow you to take a capture of the entire page, even if it exceeds the height of the laptop’s screen.

Finally, if you are an active user of social media websites, you can use Lenovo’s built-in ‘Vantage application’ to take a screenshot and quickly post it to your favorite social media sites such as Facebook, Twitter, and Reddit with only one click.

In conclusion, taking a screenshot on a Lenovo laptop with Windows 10 is easy and straightforward. Whether you want to capture an entire screen, part of it, or an active window, there are multiple easy options available to fit your needs.

2. Step-by-Step Guide on Taking Screenshots#

  1. Firstly, press down the ‘Windows’ key and the ‘Print Screen’ key simultaneously which will take a screenshot of the entire screen.
  2. After pressing the combination of keys, the screenshot will be saved automatically into the ‘Pictures’ folder in your computer.
  3. If you need to take a screenshot of a selected area of the screen, you can do that by using the Snipping Tool.
  4. On your Lenovo laptop, you can find the Snipping Tool on the ‘Start’ menu.
  5. After choosing the Snipping Tool, you will be able to select the area you want to capture.
  6. Once the area is selected, you can edit the image or save it into a folder of your choice.

3. Ways to Edit or Crop Your Screenshots#

You can edit, save, and share your screenshots with no hassle. There are two ways to edit or crop your screenshots which you can use depending on your preference. The first way is by using the Snipping Tool. Open the Start menu and search for the Snipping tool. This will give you the option to ‘Free-form’, ‘Rectangular’, ‘Window’, or ‘Full-screen’ snip. Click the mode you want to use for the screenshot. Once the screenshot is taken, a window will appear with the screenshot, and you can erase or highlight certain parts of it. The second way is by using the Paint 3D. You can open the screenshot in Paint 3D and use the edit tool to make the edits you need. You can crop the image, add text, and make other edits, then save it. With these two easy methods, you can easily edit or crop your screenshots in no time.
