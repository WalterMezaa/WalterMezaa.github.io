Whether you’re sharing a screenshot with a colleague, documenting the graphics of a game you’re playing, or preserving information for a project, this step-by-step guide will help you quickly take a screenshot on your Lenovo laptop running Windows 10.

Method 1: Take a quick screenshot with the Print Screen key

  1. Find the Print Screen key on your keyboard. This key is often abbreviated as “PrtSc” and is located at the top-right corner of your keyboard. Pressing this key will take a snapshot of your entire screen and save it to your clipboard.

  2. Open an image editing program such as Microsoft Paint or Paint 3D. You can find these programs by going to the Windows search bar.

  3. Paste the screenshot to the image editor. To do this, click on the “Paste” icon in the top menu bar, or press “Ctrl” + “V” keys on the keyboard.

  4. Resize, crop, or edit the screenshot as needed.

  5. Save the image file with the desired title and file format.

Method 2: Take a selective screenshot with the Snipping Tool

  1. Open the Snipping Tool program by going to the Windows search bar and typing “Snipping Tool”.

  2. Select the type of screenshot you want to take. You can choose to take a rectangular snip, a free-form snip, a window snip, or a full-screen snip.

  3. Once you have the desired area of your screen selected, the snip will open in a new window. You can edit it, add annotations, or share it with someone directly from the Snipping Tool window.

  4. If you’d like to save the image, go to the “File” menu and select “Save As”. Then, choose a file name and the desired file format before clicking “Save”.

1. What is Screenshot#

A screenshot is an image captured of your computer or laptop’s screen. It is also known as a print screen or screengrab. This type of image can be used to show error messages or show a colleague a view of your screen. Taking screenshots on a Lenovo laptop running Windows 10 is easy to do and this guide will show you the different ways you can get your desired screenshot.

The most convenient way to take a screenshot is by using the Windows + PrtScn keyboard shortcut. When you press both keys at the same time the screenshot will be taken and automatically saved to your Pictures > Screenshots folder.

Alternatively, you can use the Snipping tool to take a screenshot. This tool is pre-installed on all Windows computers and can be found in the Start menu. It allows you to select certain parts of the screen to capture and also provides more screenshot options such as free form, rectangular, window, or full-screen snip.

Another option is the “Share” feature which allows you to quickly take a screenshot and share it on social media. Located next to the Delete and Caps Lock keys, the share button allows you to take a screenshot and quickly share it on your favourite social media platform.

You can also use a third party application to take screenshots. Snagit is a great tool that allows you to capture an individual window, a region of the screen, or the entire screen. This application also allows you to edit and enhance your screenshots and create arrows and text boxes to emphasize important details.

Screenshots are a great way to capture what’s on your screen and can be used to help colleagues, friends, and family with their computer related issues. Hopefully, this tutorial has shown you how to quickly and easily take a screenshot on your Lenovo laptop running Windows 10.

2. How to Take a Screenshot on a Lenovo Laptop Windows 10#

  1. Go to the screen or window that you want to take a screenshot of.
  2. Press the Fn key and the PrtScn key simultaneously on your Lenovo laptop. This activates Lenovo’s Quick Capture feature.
  3. To take a screenshot of the whole screen, press the Windows key + PrtScn.
  4. The ‘screenshot’ of the active window will be automatically saved in the Pictures folder, in the subfolder labelled as ‘Screenshots’.
  5. To open the screenshot, go to Pictures > Screenshots.
  6. The screenshots can also be accessed via the Windows’ built-in Photos app.

3. Tools You’ll Need to Take a Screenshot on a Lenovo Laptop Windows 10#

If you own a Lenovo laptop running Windows 10, taking a screenshot is easy. All you need to do is press the Windows key and the Print Screen key at the same time. This will take a full capture of your entire screen. To store the screenshot, press the Windows key and the letter ‘E’. This will open a window which shows below your Pictures, Documents, Music and Video folders. Navigate to the folder labelled ‘Screenshots’, and you’ll find your screenshot that you just took.

If you’d like to capture just the active window, instead press Alt and Print Screen at the same time. This will take a screenshot of just the window which is in focus. This screenshot will also be saved into the Screenshots folder.

If you’d like to take a screenshot and paste it directly into an email or document of your choice, press Windows Key + Shift + S. This will open a feature called ‘Snipping.’ This feature will allow you to drag your cursor around an item on your screen and take a screenshot of it. You then have the option to copy this screenshot and paste it into another program.

To take a longer screenshot, meaning longer than the width of your screen, press Windows Key + Shift + Enter. This will open a feature called ‘Delay.’ Here, you can choose a timer that will allow you to navigate to a website, application, or document and take a longer screenshot.

To take a screenshot of an active window and paste it into your clipboard, press Windows Key + Alt + Print Screen. This will save your screenshot to the clipboard, and from there you can paste it into the document of your choice.

To capture a full-screen video of your desktop, press Windows Key + G. Then, press the record button which looks like a circle with a dot in the center of it. To stop recording at any time, press the record button again. The video will be saved into your Videos folder.

4. Where Your Screenshots are Saved on a Lenovo Laptop Windows 10#

To take a screenshot on a Lenovo laptop running Windows 10, press the Windows and PrtScn keys at the same time. This will capture a screenshot of the full screen. The image will be automatically saved in the Photos app located in the Pictures folder, under Screenshots. To customize the screenshot, you can use the built-in Snipping Tool. This tool allows you to take a screenshot of a specific area of the screen, a window, or the whole screen. After taking the screenshot, the image can be found in the same location as the full screen screenshot. Alternatively, you can use the Alt+PrtScn shortcut to take a screenshot of the active window. This image will also be saved in the Photos app, located in the Pictures folder, under Screenshots.
