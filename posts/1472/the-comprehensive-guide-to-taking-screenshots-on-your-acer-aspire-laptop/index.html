Q1: What is a screenshot? A1: A screenshot is an image of whatever is currently being displayed on a computer’s screen, taken in an instant.

Q2: What is the easiest way to take a screenshot on my Acer Aspire laptop? A2: The easiest way to take a screenshot on an Acer Aspire laptop is by using keyboard shortcuts. To capture the entire screen of your laptop, press the ‘Windows Key+Shift+S’. To capture only the active window, press the ‘Alt+PrtScn’.

Q3: How can I take a screenshot and save it as a file? A3: To take a screenshot and save it as a file, press the ‘Windows Key+PrtScn’ and then check your Pictures folder in File Explorer. There you can find a newly created screenshot folder containing your screenshot.

Q4: What if I want to paste a screenshot in an email or instant messaging application? A4: To paste a screenshot in an email or instant messaging application, press ‘Alt+PrtScn’ and then paste it as you would normally do in an email or IM application by using the Ctrl+V keyboard shortcut.

Q5: What if I want to edit my screenshot before saving? A5: To edit your screenshot before saving, press ‘Windows Key+Shift+S’ and then select the area of the screenshot that you want to capture. Once you’ve marked the area, it will be copied to the clipboard and you can paste it in any image editing application such as Paint or GIMP.
