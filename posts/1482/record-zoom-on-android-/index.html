1. Preparation for Recording Zoom Meetings#

1.Before starting to record, make sure that you have the latest version of Zoom installed on your Android tablet. 2.Log in to your Zoom account on your Android tablet, press the New Meeting button, and enter the meeting ID you were provided with. 3.To start recording your Zoom meeting, press the Record icon on the navigation bar at the bottom of your screen. 4.During the meeting, you can pause or mute the recording as needed. 5.When the meeting has ended, hit the Record icon once again to stop the recording. 6.The recorded file will then be saved to the Zoom folder on your Android tablet.

2. Launch Zoom Meeting on Your Android Tablet#

  1. Open the Zoom app on your Android tablet. Sign in to your account with your email address and password.
  2. Once you have logged in, tap the New Meeting button to start a call.
  3. On the next screen enter the Meeting ID and tap “Join”.
  4. You will be asked to allow access to your device’s microphone and camera. Select Allow.
  5. You will now be greeted by the other participants who have joined the meeting.
  6. You may now start recording by tapping the Record icon on the bottom right of your screen. The video will automatically be saved in the “Zoom” folder on your tablet.
  7. When you have finished the meeting, tap the Record icon once again to stop the recording. The video will be saved in the “Zoom” folder on the tablet.

3. Enable Audio & Video Options#

  1. Before joining the Zoom meeting, you can enable audio and video options on your Android tablet. To do this, tap the Settings icon at the top-right corner of the Zoom window and go to the ‘Audio’ tab. Here, you can select if you want to join the meeting with or without video, and if you want to use your device’s mic or a headset. Additionally, you can test your audio settings before joining the meeting. Once you’ve set up audio, you can switch to the ‘Video’ tab and choose which camera to use for the video call. You can also check to see if the video is working correctly in the ‘Video Preview’ section.

4. Record the Meeting with Zoom Recording Tool#

  1. Launch Zoom App on your Android tablet and log into your account.
  2. Upon launching the app, tap on “New Meeting” icon to start a new meeting. Enter the meeting ID into the required field and tap “Join Meeting”.
  3. Once inside the meeting, tap on the “Record” button at the bottom left of the screen to begin recording the meeting. The app will generate an audio and video file of the meeting that will be downloaded to your device’s “Zoom” folder.
  4. To stop recording, tap on the “Record” button again. The app will then prompt you to save the file.
  5. Tap on “Save” at the top right corner of the screen to save the recording. You can now access the recording from the device’s “Zoom” folder.
  6. Finally, to delete the recording, open the file and tap on “Delete” at the top right corner of the screen. The file will then be permanently removed from your device.

Q1. How can I record my Zoom meetings on my Android tablet? A1. To record your Zoom meetings on your Android tablet, you first need to open the Zoom App and select the “Record” option from the menu. Then, select your recording preferences, such as the participants, audio and video quality, to configure your recording. Once you have configured your recording, tap the “Start” button to begin the recording.

Q2. What settings should I use to ensure the best recording quality? A2. To ensure the best recording quality, you should choose the highest video and audio quality settings, as well as the “Best” option for the participants. Additionally, make sure to turn off any visual effects that reduce video quality, such as the Zoom virtual background feature.

Q3. How can I find the recording once it has been completed? A3. Once your Zoom meeting recording has completed, you can open the Zoom App and select the “Recordings” option from the menu. This will bring up a list of all your recordings, each one with a unique name. You can then select the recording you wish to play.

Q4. What video file format will my recording be in? A4. Your Zoom recording will be saved as an MP4 file. This is a widely supported format, which allows you to easily share and distribute the recording to other viewers.

Q5. How can I share my recording with other participants? A5. To share your recording with other participants, first open the Zoom App and select the “Share” option from the menu. You can then select the recording you wish to share and enter the recipient’s email address. The recipient will then receive an email containing a link to the recording.
