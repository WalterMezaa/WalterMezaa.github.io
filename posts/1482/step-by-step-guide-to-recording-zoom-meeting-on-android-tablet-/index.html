Record Zoom on Android

  1. Log into your Zoom account
  2. Open the settings and tap on the Recording tab
  3. Tap on the option for Enable Recording
  4. Select the option for Local Recording
  5. Go to the meeting that you want to record and tap the “Record” button
  6. Wait for the recording to start
  7. Pause and resume the recording when needed
  8. When the meeting is done, tap the “Stop” button
  9. A dialog box will appear with the recording saved on your tablet
  10. Tap the “Share” button to export the recording to your computer or cloud storage

2. Open and Sign into Zoom App#

  1. Download Zoom app from Google Play Store and open it.

  3. Go to the Settings page and tap on the Recording tab.
  4. Enable Recording and set Local Recording.

3. Start & Join Meeting#

  1. To start the recording, log into your Zoom account on your Android tablet.
  2. Tap the settings icon at the bottom right and select the ‘Recording’ tab.
  3. Here, enable recording to be able to record the meeting. Select ‘Local Recording’ if you would like to save the video on your tablet.
  4. Then, open the meeting you would like to record and tap ‘Record’ to start the recording.
  5. You can pause/resume the recording at any time from the meeting controls.
  6. When you’re done, tap ‘Stop’ to end the recording. A dialog box will appear with the recorded session.

4. Record Meeting#

  1. To record a meeting on an Android tablet, the first step is to open the Zoom app and log in to your pre-existing Zoom account.
  2. Once logged in, you’ll be taken to the main page of the app. From here, open Settings by clicking on the gear icon at the top of the page.
  3. Within Settings, you’ll be given a list of options and settings to adjust. Tap the Recording tab to begin adjusting the recording settings.
  4. After entering the Recording tab, turn on the local recording function by toggling the switch to the on position.
  5. Now that the recording options are set, it’s time to join the meeting. Once you’re in the meeting, you can tap the record button, which will be located at the bottom of the meeting window.
  6. During the meeting you can pause and resume the recording as needed, however, remember to tap the Stop button once the meeting is concluded.
  7. After clicking the Stop button, a dialog box will appear before the recording is saved. From here you’ll be able to adjust the settings and settings before saving.
