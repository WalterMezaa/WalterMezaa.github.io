Step by Step Guide to Recording Zoom Meeting on Android Tablet Are you looking for an easy way to record a Zoom meeting on an Android tablet? This guide will show you the steps to do so.

First, open the Zoom app on your tablet. You can find it under ‘Apps’ in the Android menu. When you open the app, sign in with your account or create a new one if you do not have one.

Once you are logged in, launch the meeting you wish to record. When you are in the meeting, tap the ‘More’ option. This will open a drop-down menu. In this menu, select the ‘Record’ option.

Once you have selected the ‘Record’ option, you will be asked to confirm the recording. Tap ‘Yes’ to begin recording the meeting. You can also choose to record the audio only or video and audio both.

At the bottom of the screen, you will find the ‘Stop Recording’ button. Tap on this button to end the recording. Once the recording is complete, you will get a notification saying ‘Recording stopped’.

That’s it, you have successfully recorded a Zoom meeting on your Android tablet. Now you can upload the recorded video to your YouTube or any other streaming service, or share it with your colleagues.

4. Start Recording the Meeting with Your Android Tablet#

  1. Firstly, make sure you have the Zoom app installed and open it on your Android tablet.
  2. Log in with your Zoom ID, and start the virtual meeting you want to record.
  3. When you are in the meeting, you should be able to see the Record button at the bottom of the page.
  4. Tap the Record button, It may take a few seconds before the recording begins.
  5. When you wish to stop the recording, press the Stop button. The file will automatically be saved.
