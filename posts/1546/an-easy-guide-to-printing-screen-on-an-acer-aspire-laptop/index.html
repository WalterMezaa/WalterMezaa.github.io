A Step by Step Guide on How to Print Screen on an Acer Aspire Laptop

How to Quickly Take a Screen Shot on an Acer Aspire Laptop

Take Screenshot Acer Aspire

  1. To print your screen, press the ‘PrtScr (Print Screen)’ key on your keyboard.
  2. Once the PrtScr key is pressed, an image of the current screen will be saved to your clipboard.
  3. Open an image editing software, such as Microsoft Paint.
  4. Press ‘Ctrl + V’ to paste the image in the software.
  5. Use the tools in the software to crop the image to your desired area.
  6. Once cropped, you can save the image or you can choose to print it.
  7. To do this, select ‘File’ and then ‘Print’ from the dropdown menu.
  8. Your printer settings will then appear. Choose your desired settings and then hit ‘Print’.
  9. The screenshot will then be printed out from your Acer Aspire laptop.

1. How to Prepare the Printer#

Before using the PrtScr (Print Screen) button on your Acer Aspire Laptop, it is best to prepare your printer and your software beforehand. Ensure that the printer is properly connected to your laptop and the ink cartridge have been installed. If necessary, download the driver for the printer or ensure that your computer has the latest driver update for the printer. You can also open the software that you will use to edit the print screen snapshot, like Microsoft Paint. When all of these are set, you can now start taking the print screen snapshot.

2. Steps to Print Screen#

To take a print screen on an Acer Aspire laptop, the first step is to locate and press the ‘PrtScr (Print Screen)’ button in the keyboard. The second step is to open an image editing software such as Microsoft Paint. The third step is to press ‘Ctrl + V’ to paste the screenshot into the software. The fourth step is to use the cropping tool available in the software to only capture the desired area. The fifth step is to save the screenshot in the format and location of choice. If further editing is necessary, the photo can be further edited in the same software. Finally, the screenshot can be shared or viewed for review.

3. What to Do if Printer Fails#

If the printer fails, you may need to try reinstalling the device driver. To do this, open the Windows Device Manager and choose the printer you need. Right click and select Uninstall, then reboot your laptop and reinstall the printer driver. You may also need to check if the printer connection is secure or if the cables are seated properly. If there are no physical issues, you can try running a troubleshooting scan to identify the problem and find the right solution. If you are still unable to resolve the issue, you can contact the customer support for further instructions.

4. How to Troubleshoot a Printer Problem#

If the printer is not responding, then the first step should be to check to see that the printer is plugged in and the power switch is on. Secondly, make sure that the printer is installed on the computer properly. It is also important to check for any loose wires or plugs that may be causing the malfunction. Additionally, ensure that the printer drivers are up to date and compatible with the computer. If all else fails, contact the printer manufacturer support for further troubleshooting.
