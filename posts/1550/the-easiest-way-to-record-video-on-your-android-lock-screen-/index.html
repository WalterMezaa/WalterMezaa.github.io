Step 2: Lock your device Press the power button to lock your device. Once the device is locked, you will notice that the camera app is still running in the background.

Step 3: Activate video recording Press and hold the power button and, while keeping your finger pressed, swipe up to bring up the camera app. Once the app pops up, release your finger and your video recording will begin immediately.

Step 4: Record your video Record the video you wish to capture.
