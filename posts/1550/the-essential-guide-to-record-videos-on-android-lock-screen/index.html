  2. Enable USB Debugging: You will need to enable USB Debugging mode on your Android device in order to record videos from the lock screen. To enable USB Debugging, open the Settings menu on your device, then select ‘Developer Options’ and then ‘USB Debugging.’

  3. Connect your device: Connect your Android device to your computer using a USB cable. Make sure that it is securely connected and that your device is recognized by your computer.
