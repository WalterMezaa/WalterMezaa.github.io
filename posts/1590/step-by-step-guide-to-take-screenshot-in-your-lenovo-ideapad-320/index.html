1. Introduction to Lenovo IdeaPad 320#

Lenovo IdeaPad 320 is a great laptop that comes with lots of functionalities that make it easy for you to take screenshots. You can easily take a screenshot of any screen with the help of the two buttons located on the left side of your keyboard. All you need to do is press Fn and Print Screen buttons simultaneously. After doing so, the screenshot will be saved in the ‘Screenshots’ folder in the device. You can open this folder to view, rename, share or even edit the screenshot using Paint. It is a very useful tool and you can use it to take screenshots with ease.

2. Ways to Take Screenshot in Lenovo IdeaPad 320#

  1. On your Lenovo IdeaPad 320 laptop, locate and press the ‘Fn’ key along with the ‘Print Screen’ key to capture a screenshot.
  2. The captured image will be saved in the ‘Screenshots’ folder of your laptop automatically.
  3. To open the ‘Screenshots’ folder, go to the ‘Pictures’ section followed by access to the ‘Screenshots’ folder.
  4. Once you have opened the ‘Screenshots’ folder, you can view, rename, share or edit the images as per your choice.
  5. You can also use the Windows Paint program to edit the screenshots.
  6. To share the captured screenshot, simply copy and paste the image in emails or social media platforms.

3. Useful Tips for Taking Screenshot in Lenovo IdeaPad 320#

  1. Firstly, locate the function (Fn) key on your laptop’s keyboard. It is usually positioned on the lower left side of the keyboard, along with other keys like Ctrl or Alt.
  2. Now, once you have got the function key, press it in combination with the Print Screen (PrtSc) key.
  3. It will trigger a screenshot capture and the resulting image will be automatically saved in the ‘Screenshots’ folder in the Pictures library of your Lenovo IdeaPad 320.
  4. To view and manage the taken screenshot, simply open the folder and you can preview, rename, delete or share the image as needed.
  5. In addition, you can also edit the screenshot using the Windows Paint or any other image editing software installed on your device.
  6. Lastly, you can even set up a hotkey of your own, for the purpose of taking screenshots in your Lenovo IdeaPad 320. To do this, you need to go to your laptop’s settings, click on the ‘Keyboards’ option and then set a combination of keys or a single key to act as a hotkey.

4. Conclusion#

After you have successfully taken the screenshot, you can open it in a photo editing application like Paint to make further changes. Another option is to share it with friends online or rename it according to your own requirement. For future references, all screenshots taken on the Lenovo IdeaPad 320 will be saved in the Screenshots folder. Q1.What is a screenshot? A1.A screenshot is an image taken of whatever is on your computer screen.

Q2.How to take a screenshot on my Lenovo IdeaPad 320? A2.Press the Windows logo key + PrtScn. The screen shot is saved to the Pictures Library, in the Screenshots folder.

Q3.How can I capture only the active window? A3.Press Alt + PrtScn. The active window will be copied to the clipboard.

Q4.How do I take a delayed screenshot? A4.Press the Windows logo key + Shift + S. Your screen will appear grayed out and your mouse cursor will change. Drag the cursor over the area you want to capture. When you release your mouse button, the screenshot will be saved to your clipboard.

Q5.How do I share a screenshot? A5.If you’ve taken a screenshot, you can paste it into an email or another program. To do this, press the Windows logo key + V. If the screenshot is saved to your Pictures Library, you can also share it from there.
