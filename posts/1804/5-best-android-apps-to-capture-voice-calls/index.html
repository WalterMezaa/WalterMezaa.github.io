3. Detailed Discussion of the Apps#

The Automatic Call Recorder allows users to easily keep track of the recordings and easily access the recordings made. This app enables users to record their incoming and outgoing calls without sending any notification to the other party. Also, this app enables users to organize, back up, and protect their recordings with a secure password.

The Call Recorder app enables users to record their conversations in both directions. The app also supports cloud storage services which enable them to store their recordings securely. Furthermore, this app also provides users with the ability to share recordings with other users via text, email, or social media.

The Boldbeast Recorder is one of the best Android apps to capture voice calls. This app is capable of recording all incoming and outgoing calls automatically. This app also comes with a host of features such as the ability to assign different recording amounts for different contacts, record in stereo, and even record without using up too much device storage.

The Cube Call Recorder ACR app is one of the few apps that enables users to record calls from VoIP applications like Skype, Viber, WhatsApp and more. This app provides users with the ability to record calls, save recordings securely, and share with other users with ease.

The ACR Call Recorder app allows users to record conversations in both directions. This app also features an advanced automatic deletion system which enables users to save recordings securely and automatically clear the recordings after a specific period of time. Finally, this app also supports sharing of call recordings with other users over social media.

4. Summary of the Pros and Cons of the Top Apps#

The Automatic Call Recorder apps is the simplest to use and can be considered as one of the most basic phones of the bunch. It works well with most of the Android devices and allows you to easily record conversation on both the incoming and outgoing calls. It also saves the recordings automatically to the device storage. The downside is that the recordings can’t be shared and it also consumes a lot of battery power.

The Call Recorder app records incoming and outgoing calls automatically and allows you to keep the recordings as long as you like as it doesn’t have a limit to the storage time. While it records clearly, it doesn’t offer an option to share the recorded conversations. It also supports many languages and can record incoming and outgoing calls from different phone numbers.

The Boldbeast Recorder app is a great tool for capturing recordings as it offers various features that are not available in other apps. It can save both incoming and outgoing calls separately and you can also choose to share the recordings or backup them to your device. It does not have any time limit on recordings either. On the downside, it requires root access and also consumes a great deal of battery power.

The Cube Call Recorder ACR is quite easy to use with an interactive design and it can save both incoming and outgoing call recordings automatically. It also supports voice activation, so you can start recording conversations with uttering a few words. It also supports secure cloud storage to store or share the recordings. However, it does not support call between two different phones, which is a limitation.

Finally, the ACR Call Recorder app is considered as one of the most advanced call recording apps in the market. It has a variety of features such as password protection, recording formats options and numerous cloud storage services support. It also supports multiple languages and has the ability to record both incoming and outgoing calls. The downside is that it is not free.
