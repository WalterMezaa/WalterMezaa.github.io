2. Prepare Your Recording Device and Connect it to Your TV Box#

First, plug the HDMI cable of the TV Box into the HDMI port of your TV. Then turn on the TV Box and use the remote control to find the signal source and select HDMI. Afterwards, connect one end of the microphone cable to the MicroUSB port on the back side of the TV Box, and the other end to the microphone itself. To use the microphone and record amazing content, go to the Settings menu, select the Sound & display section and check the box next to “Microphone” to enable it. That’s it! You can now use the microphone to record content on your Android TV Box. Make sure to adjust the audio settings in the display panel to ensure the best sound quality. Finally, press the microphone button on your remote control to start recording.

3. Use the TV Box Software and Start Recording#

To record amazing content with your Android TV box, next you have to use the TV box software and start recording. To do this, you need to open the TV box software and select the recording feature. Once you have selected the recording feature, you can set the audio source and select the microphone as your audio source. Then you have to set the save destination of your recordings. You can choose the internal storage or an external device. Once you have finished setting up the recording, you can start recording content. Additionally, you can also monitor the recording process and make any necessary audio adjustments as needed. After you have finished recording, you can save and store the recordings until you want to watch or share them.
