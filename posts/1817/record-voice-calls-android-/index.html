3. Steps on How to Record Voice Calls on Android Phone.#

  1. Open Google Play Store on your Android device and search for ‘call recorder’.
  2. Select the app you would like to use to record calls and click ‘Install’ to download it to your device.
  3. Once the installation is complete, open the app and setup your recording preferences. Most apps will give you the option to record all calls automatically, record calls manually, or even select which contacts you would like to record calls with.
  4. Once your preferences are saved, you are now ready to start recording voice calls. To record a conversation, just make or answer a call, and click the record button provided by the recording app of your choice.
  5. Once the call is finished, the recording will automatically be stored in the app’s history or recordings list. You can listen to the audio file at any time or even send it over to another device.
  6. If you want to delete the recordings, simply open the app and tap on the file you want to delete. You will be given the option to delete the file or save it on your device.
