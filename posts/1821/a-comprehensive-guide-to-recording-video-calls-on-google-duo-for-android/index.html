Step By Step Guide to Record Google Duo Video Calls on Android

Fuss Free Ways to Capture Google Duo Video Calls on Android

Record Google Duo Videos To start recording a video call on Google Duo for Android, open the Duo app and make a video call. Once the connection has been established, tap the “More” button (three-dots icon) in the top right corner of the screen. This will show you a list of options. Choose the “Record” option.

You will then be asked to confirm that you want to record the video call by tapping on the “Record” button. Once you tap the “Record” button, the call will start recording. You can then continue to talk with your video call participants while the call is being recorded.

To end the recording, tap the “More” button (three-dots icon) again. This will show you the same list of options. Choose the “Stop Recording” option. This will stop the recording and send a copy of the recorded video call to your device.

The video call recording will be stored in your Duo app’s records. To access it, open the Duo app and tap on the “More” button (three-dots icon). This will show you a list of options. Choose the “Records” option. Here, you can find the video call recording and share it with others.
