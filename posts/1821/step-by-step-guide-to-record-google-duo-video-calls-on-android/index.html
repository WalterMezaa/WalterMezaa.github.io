Record Google Duo Videos

  1. Open the Google Duo app on your Android device.
  2. Start a video call with the person you wish to record the call with.
  3. Swipe up from the bottom of the screen and select the “Record” icon, which appears as a small circle with a line in the middle.
  4. The other party on the call will be notified that you are recording the call, and you will receive an alert that the call is being recorded.
  5. Once the call is finished, the recorded file will be automatically saved in the Google Duo folder, which can be accessed from within the app.
  6. You can also access the recorded video from the Android device’s local storage, usually within the “Videos” folder.
  7. All recordings will remain stored on your device until you delete them.

Q3. What file format are recorded Google Duo Videos saved in? A3. All recorded Google Duo videos are saved in MP4 format.

Q4. Is there a limit on how long I can record a Google Duo video call? A4. No, there is no set limit on recording time. You can keep recording the video call for as long as you like.
