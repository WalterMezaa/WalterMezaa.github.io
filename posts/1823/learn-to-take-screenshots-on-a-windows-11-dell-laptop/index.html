How to Capture Screenshots on a Windows 11 Dell Laptop

Step by Step Screenshot Dell

A Guide to Taking Screenshots on a Windows 11 Dell Laptop Step 1: Locate the Print Screen Key on Your Keyboard On your keyboard, you will find a key labeled “PrtSc” or “Print Screen.” This is the key that you will use to take a screenshot of your Dell Laptop running Windows 11.

Step 2: Prepare Your Screen If you want to capture the entire display, press and hold the Windows key at the same time as you press the Print Screen key. This will capture the entire screen. If you only want to capture the active window, press Alt + Print Screen instead.

Step 3: Open the Paint Program Open the Paint program, which can be found within the Windows Accessories folder.

Step 4: To Paste a Screenshot Go to the Paint program, and press Ctrl + V or select Edit > Paste from the top menu. This will paste the screenshot that you took.

Step 5: Save Your Screenshot Click File > Save As and save your screenshot in the desired format. The screenshot will now be saved in the folder that you specified.

1. Understand the Basics of Taking a Screenshot#

Taking a screenshot is a relatively easy task to accomplish on a Windows 11 Dell laptop. To begin, press and hold the Windows key and then simultaneously press the Print Screen key. This will capture the entire display of your Dell laptop. The captured image will be stored in a folder which can be viewed in the Photos app. Additionally, you can also paste the screenshot directly into any program such as Microsoft Word or Paint. To do so, press the Windows key and the Print Screen key at the same time and then paste in the appropriate program. If you want to capture only part of the screen, press the Windows key, Shift key, and the S key. This will prompt a cursor that will allow you to highlight and capture a certain area of the screen. The screenshot will then be automatically saved in the same folder as the full screenshots. Finally, you can also take screenshots using the Snipping Tool. To access this, press the Windows key and then search for the Snipping Tool. Choose the type of snip you want to take and then click on the “New” button to select the area of your screen you wish to capture. Once done, click the “Save” button to save the screenshot in the desired location.

2. Learn How to Cut and Paste Your Screenshot onto Another File#

Once you have captured your Dell laptop’s entire display, you can easily cut and paste it onto another file. To do so, simply open the image capturing program on your Dell laptop, select the screenshot you have taken, click on the ‘Edit’ icon, then choose the ‘Cut’ option. This will cut the screenshot out of the image editor and allow you to paste the image on any document or file. You can also copy the screenshot, which will create a duplicate of the same image. To do this, select the image, click the ‘Edit’ icon, then choose the ‘Copy’ option. Then open the desired document or file, click on the ‘Edit’ icon, and choose the ‘Paste’ option, to paste the screenshot onto the file. Alternatively, you can directlysave the screenshot as a JPEG, BMP, or PNG file. This will save the image to your laptop’s hard drive, so that you can later include it in another file.

3. Discover Different Types of Screenshots#

In addition, you can capture just the active window by pressing Alt + Print Screen. This is a great option to quickly capture a specific window while you are working on other applications. If you need a quick and easy way to capture a region of the screen, press the Windows key and the letter “S” to open the snipping tool. Here you can draw and adjust shape to capture your desired area. You can also delay the capture up to five seconds. Just click the down arrow next to “New” and select the amount of time the delay will last before the window is captured. All the screenshots taken will be saved in your Pictures folder. If you need to capture just a small portion of the screen, pressing the Windows key and “Shift” key with the “S” brings up an option to draw and capture the area that is most important to you. Lastly, you can also capture all the opened windows (for example to list all the programs you have opened) by pressing the Windows key and the “T” key. This will result in a screenshot with all the programs listed for you.

4. Find Out How to Save Your Screenshot#

After pressing both of the keys, a screenshot will be saved to the Screenshots folder that can be found in Pictures library. You can access this folder using File Explorer. On the left pane, select ‘This PC’ followed by changing the folder view to ‘Pictures’. The screenshot will be saved with the ‘Screenshot’ label and a number starting from 1. To keep the screenshot intact, remember to save it in a different folder.

If you want to take a screenshot of an area, press the Windows + Shift + S keys. Your screen will dim and a cursor appears, which you can use to drag and take a screenshot of the desired area. After you take a screenshot, a notification will appear at the bottom-right corner of the screen that allows you to copy the capture and access the screenshot from the clipboard.

You can also use the Windows Game Bar to capture a screenshot. Press Windows+G to open the Windows Game Bar and click the camera icon on the bar, which will allow you to take a screenshot that will be automatically saved in the Captures folder. You can locate this folder inside the ‘Videos’ library.

Moreover, to enhance your screenshot, you can open the screenshot with the Photos app. On the top ribbon, click ‘Edit and Create’ to access the editing tools. The editing tools include cropping, filtering, and enhancing the photo fade and contrast. Additionally, you can also draw and add text to the screenshot.

Finally, to save your screenshot to different formats, right-click on the screenshot to access the ‘Save as’ option. You can save the screenshot as a .jpeg, .png, .gif, .webp, or Bitmap. Select the format of the image you want and click ‘Save’ and your screenshot will be saved in the selected format.

Q1: What is a screenshot? A1: A screenshot is a digital image of what is visible on a computer monitor, television, or other visual output device.

Q2: How do I take a screenshot on a Windows 11 Dell laptop? A2: To take a screenshot on a Windows 11 Dell laptop, you will first need to press the “PrtScn” key on your laptop. This will capture a screenshot of everything currently visible on your laptop’s screen.

Q3: How do I save a screenshot? A3: Once you have taken the screenshot, you can save it on your laptop by pressing the “Windows + PrtScn” keys together. This will save the screenshot as an image file in the “Screenshots” folder of your Pictures folder.

Q4: How do I copy a screenshot? A4: To copy a screenshot, press the “Ctrl + PrtScn” keys together. This will copy the screenshot to your clipboard, which you can then paste into any other application, such as an image editing software or even a text editor.

Q5: How do I edit a screenshot? A5: To edit a screenshot, open the file using your preferred image editing software. From here, you can crop, resize, add text, add shapes, and perform other adjustments to your screenshot.
