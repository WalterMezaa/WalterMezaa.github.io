I. Introduction#

Once you have the Screen Recorder app on your Samsung tablet, you can follow the instructions in this guide to start recording your tablet’s screen. First, you need to launch the app. Then you will be presented with a ‘Start Recording’ button which you need to select in order to begin recording. If you need to mute the sound, you can select the ‘Mute’ button. Otherwise, you can select ‘Record Internal Sound’ in order to record the sound from your device. Additionally, you can set the timer for desired duration of recording by selecting ‘Recording Time’.

II. Prerequisite#

Before beginning the recording, it is important to have a few things ready beforehand. First, the ‘Screen Recorder’ app must be downloaded and installed on the Samsung tablet. It is available for free on either the App Store or Google Play. Additionally, the user should make sure the microphone is muted and that the sound level is at a suitable level. Lastly, the user should check that the tablet is plugged in and that the battery is at an appropriate level. Having these components ready will ensure a smooth process for recording the screen.

III. Step-by-Step Guide#

To begin with, unlock your Samsung tablet and find the ‘Screen Recorder’ app. Open the app and click on the ‘Start Recording’ button to start recording your tablet screen. You may select the ‘Mute’ option if you do not want to record the sound or choose the ‘Record Internal Sound’ option to record the device’s sound. If you want to set a timer for only a specific duration, you may use the ‘Recording Time’ option to set the desired time limit for recording. As soon as you are done with the recording, you may stop the recording by pressing the ‘Stop Recording’ button and save the recording.

Here are few quick tips when recording your tablet screen. Make sure you set the resolution and size as per the quality and size as it can affect the output quality and size of the recording. You should also cover all the areas you want to record in one go as recording in multiple sessions may lead to synchronization issues. IV. Conclusion#

  1. After launching the ‘Screen Recorder’ app, users can adjust the settings to their preference such as resolution of recording, audio control (mute/record internal sound) and recording timer.
  2. When the settings are done, the user can click on ‘Start Recording’ and a 3-second countdown will follow before the recording begins.
  3. To pause the recording, a simple press of the pause button will freeze the recording.
  4. To resume the recording, the user can simply hit the resume button.
  5. For recordings longer than 10 minutes, user is strongly recommended to plug their Samsung tablet to an external power source to prevent any disruption due to low battery.
  6. Upon completion of recording, users can click on the ‘Stop Recording’ button to save the video and if they are pleased with it they can also share it with others via various social media platforms or cloud applications.
