3. How to Recover WhatsApp Chat History from Local Backups#

First, locate your local backup files. Depending on your device settings, they can be found at either sdcard/WhatsApp/Databases/ or Internal Storage/WhatsApp/Databases/. Once you’ve located it, transfer the file to a computer.

Then, create a folder using any name and download a file manager application, such as WinRAR for Windows or Unarchiver for Mac. The file manager app will help you to extract the backup file and view its content.

Third, open the file manager app and select the file you extracted in the previous step. Then, open the WhatsApp folder and find the backup files.

Fourth, create a new folder in the WhatsApp folder and name it ‘Restore’. Then, copy all the chat history files and paste them in the ‘Restore’ folder.

After that, reinstall WhatsApp on your device and open it. Go to the ‘Restore’ folder in the WhatsApp folder and select the oldest backup file. Finally, your chat history will be restored.

In addition, if you wish to restore your backed up media files, you can do so by moving the ‘media’ folder to the root of your device after you’ve copied the ‘Restore’ folder in the WhatsApp folder.

4. How to Restore WhatsApp Chat History with Google Drive#

  1. Backup your chat conversations via Google Drive. Open WhatsApp and head to Settings/Chats/Chat backup. Tap on the ‘Back up to Google Drive’ option.
  2. Select the frequency for backups, such as ‘Daily’ or ‘Monthly’ depending on your usage.
  3. When the backup is enabled, it will automatically start but you can also choose to ‘Back up now’ to start it immediately.
  4. Once the backup is complete, uninstall WhatsApp from your device. Note that this will delete all the messages from your device.
  5. After uninstalling WhatsApp, reinstall it from the Play Store.
  6. During the process of reinstalling, you will be prompted to restore your chat history from Google Drive. Select ‘Restore’ and your chats will be restored.
