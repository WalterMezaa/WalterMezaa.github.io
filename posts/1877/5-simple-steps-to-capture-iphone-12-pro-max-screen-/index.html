1. Turn on “AssistiveTouch” Feature#

AssistiveTouch is a feature designed to help people adjust settings with a few easy clicks or taps. To turn on this feature, go to Settings > Accessibility > Touch > AssistiveTouch, then toggle the switch. After that, a virtual button will appear on your iPhone 12 Pro Max screen. To capture your screen, press and hold the Home/Side/Volume Up buttons at the same time and release when the screen flashes. This will save a screenshot to the Photos app, which can be accessed and edited at any time. It is possible to adjust the screenshot to the size of your preference. Additionally, you can add text, draw, and more.

Furthermore, if you want to turn off the AssistiveTouch feature, just access the setting as before and toggle the switch back off. 2. Access the “Screenshot Tool”#

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone 12 Pro Max.
  2. Scroll down and tap on the “Control Center” option.
  3. Tap on the “Customize Controls” option.
  4. Scroll down and find the “Screenshot Tool” option and tap the plus icon next to it.
  5. Now you can access the “Screenshot Tool” by swiping down from the top of the screen to reveal the Control Center, then tapping the screenshot icon.
  6. After capturing a screenshot, it will be saved in the Photos app for you to view, edit, and share.

3. Capture Your iPhone 12 Pro Max Screen#

  1. To capture the screen of your iPhone 12 Pro Max, start by pressing and holding the Side and Volume Up buttons simultaneously.
  2. Continue to hold the buttons until the display flashes white in response to the button pressing.
  3. This is a sign that you have successfully initiated the screenshot feature on your device.
  4. Once the screenshot has been taken, you will be able to view the image in the Camera Roll of the Photos app.
  5. Here, you can crop and edit the image as per your needs before saving or sharing it.
  6. You can also share the screenshot to other apps such as Mail, Messages, and social media platforms.

4. Edit and Share Your Screenshot#

  1. After pressing and holding the Side and Volume Up buttons on your iPhone 12 Pro Max, your device will make a sound and your screen will flash.

  2. Your screenshot will then appear at the bottom left corner of your screen as a thumbnail. Tap the thumbnail to open it.

  3. You can then edit your screenshot by adding drawings, shapes, text, and more.

  4. After making your edits, tap the share icon to send the image via messages, mail, save to photos, upload to social media, or share it with other apps.

  5. Finally, tap Done to save the changes and finish editing your screenshot.
