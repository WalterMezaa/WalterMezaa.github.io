5 Simple Steps to Capture iPhone 12 Pro Max Screen

Screenshot in Seconds with iPhone 12 Pro Max Instructions and Guide: Get the Most Out of Your iPhone 12 Pro Max with Screenshots

  1. Open Your Device’s Screenshot Menu: Press and hold the Side button and either of the Volume buttons at the same time for a brief second.
  2. Take a Screenshot: Tap the Screenshot button located at the bottom-left corner of the menu.
  3. Edit a Screenshot: Once you’ve taken the screenshot, you can crop, add text, emojis, and more by tapping the “Edit” button located at the upper-right corner.
  4. Share or Delete a Screenshot: To share a screenshot with your contacts, tap the “Share” button and select from a variety of options such as Messages, Mail, Twitter, and more. To delete the screenshot, tap the “Delete” button located at the bottom-left corner.
  5. Save a Screenshot: To save a screenshot, tap the “Save Image” button located at the top-right corner.
  6. Open Saved Screenshots: To open a saved screenshot, tap the Photos icon located on the Home Screen, and open the “Screenshots” folder.
  7. Take a Long Screenshot: To capture a longer page, press and hold the Side button and either of the Volume buttons again. Then, tap the “Full Page” button located at the bottom-right corner.
  8. Capture a Video Recording: Open the Control Center and press and hold the Screen Recording icon. Then, select “Start Recording” and the recording will begin. To finish, tap the red bar at the top of the screen.
