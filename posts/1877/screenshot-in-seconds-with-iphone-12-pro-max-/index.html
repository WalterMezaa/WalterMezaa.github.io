Follow these simple steps to take a screenshot on your iPhone 12 Pro Max.

First, press and hold the Sleep/Wake button located at the top of your device. Then, press and quickly release the Volume Up button located on the left side of your device. Q: How do I take a screenshot on my iPhone 12 Pro Max? A: Taking a screenshot on your iPhone 12 Pro Max is a simple process that just requires a few steps. First, press and hold the side button on the right side of the device and then immediately press the Volume Up button. This will take a screenshot which will be saved to your Photos app.

Q: Can I take a screenshot on my iPhone 12 Pro Max with gestures? A: Yes, you can take a screenshot with gestures on your iPhone 12 Pro Max. You can use the AssistiveTouch menu to quickly take a screenshot. To enable AssistiveTouch, go to Settings > Accessibility > Touch > AssistiveTouch and toggle the switch on. Once enabled, you can access the AssistiveTouch menu by swiping down from the top right corner of your screen. From the menu, tap on the Device button and then press the ‘More’ button. Finally select the ‘Screenshot’ option to take a screenshot.

Q: Can I edit a screenshot I just took on my iPhone 12 Pro Max? A: Yes, you can edit a screenshot taken on your iPhone 12 Pro Max. To do so, open the screenshot in the Photos app and tap the ‘Edit’ button. This will open the image in the photo editor where you can rotate, crop, and add filters to your photo.

Q: Can I take a partial screenshot on my iPhone 12 Pro Max? A: Yes, you can take a partial screenshot on your iPhone 12 Pro Max. To do so, first take a full screenshot by pressing the Side button and Volume Up buttons simultaneously. Then open the screenshot in the Photos app and select the ‘Crop and Rotate’ button at the bottom of the screen. This will allow you to manually draw the selection for a partial screenshot.

Q: What format are screenshots taken on my iPhone 12 Pro Max? A: Screenshots taken on your iPhone 12 Pro Max are saved in JPG or PNG format. You can check the format of your screenshot by going to the Photos app and selecting the image. The image format will be listed below the screenshot.
