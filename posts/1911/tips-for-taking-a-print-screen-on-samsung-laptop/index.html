#5 When You Want to Share a Screenshot If you want to quickly share a screenshot with friends or colleagues, press the “Windows + PrtScr” keys on your keyboard. This will save an image of the entire screen in File Explorer’s Pictures library. From here you can easily share it with anyone.

1. Introduction to Print Screens#

A print screen, or screenshot, is a capture of what is displayed on your device screen. On a Samsung laptop, you can take a screenshot of a specific area, window, or of the whole screen easily. To take a screenshot on a Samsung laptop, press the Windows+Shift+S key combination. Doing so will open a toolbar allowing you to select the area of the screen you wish to take a screenshot of. Once the image has been captured, it will be saved to your clipboard. You can then paste it to any other application or folder. Taking a screenshot is a convenient way to capture a moment on the screen that you can then use for other purposes.

Alternatively, the “Snipping Tool” can be used to take screenshots. This tool is already preinstalled on all Windows machines and can be found by searching in your start menu. To access the Game Bar, press the “Windows+G” key combination on your keyboard. From there, you can choose to capture the entire screen, or just a specific region.

In conclusion, there are several different options for taking screenshots on Samsung laptops. Whether it’s through a simple key combination, the snipping tool or the game bar, you can find the right solution that suits your needs.
