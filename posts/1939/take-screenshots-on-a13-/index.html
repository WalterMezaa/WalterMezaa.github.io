  4. Find the content that you need to capture as a screenshot.

2. Types of Screenshots available#

There are two types of screenshots available on the Samsung Galaxy A13 4G, image capture and scrolling capture. Image capture allows you to take a single full-screen screenshot, while scrolling capture allows you to take a longer screenshot of the entire page that you are viewing. To take a scrolling capture, simply press the same combination of keys you would use for an image capture, but once the screenshot is taken, just keep your finger pressed down on the Power/Lock and Volume Down buttons to keep the screenshot rolling. Once you’ve reached the bottom of the page, the screenshot will automatically be saved.

3. Steps to Take Screenshots on Samsung Galaxy A13 4G#

This article will provide instructions on how to take screenshots on Samsung Galaxy A13 4G. Screenshots are useful to capture and share specific content from your device.

To take screenshots on your Samsung Galaxy A13 4G, press and hold the Power/Lock button and the Volume Down button simultaneously until you hear a shutter sound and the screen flashes to confirm a screenshot is taken. Once you have taken the screenshot, you will receive a pop-up notification confirming that the screenshot was saved.

Alternatively, you can take a screenshot by swiping your hand over the Samsung Galaxy A13 4G’s screen. To enable this feature, go to Settings > Advanced features > Motions and gestures and then switch on Palm swipe to capture. Once enabled, you can take screenshots by simply swiping the edge of your hand across the screen from one side to the other.

You can find all the screenshots you take on your device in either of the two places. First, open the Gallery app and tap the Screenshots folder to find the screenshots you have taken. Secondly, you can access the screenshots from the My Files folder in the Device Storage.

You can also edit or share the screenshot you just took. To edit the screenshot, tap the Edit button located in the bottom right corner of the screenshot. To share it, tap the Share button located in the top right corner of the screenshot. Here, you can select a platform on which you wish to share the screenshot.

In conclusion, taking screenshots on the Samsung Galaxy A13 4G is easy and convenient. Follow the instructions provided above to capture and share your screen on a Samsung Galaxy A13 4G.
