Step 1: On the Surface Laptop 4, press the Windows logo key and the volume down key simultaneously.

Step 2: The display will dim for a second and a screenshot of the entire display will be saved in the “Screenshots” folder on your Picture library.

Step 3: You can find the screenshot by going to the Start Menu, select Pictures, select Screenshots, and select the screenshot you just took.

Step 4: The screenshot will be labeled as “Screenshot (number)” and will have a .png extension. You can edit it, share it, or delete it.

Step 5: To share the screenshot, select the picture and click the Share button at the bottom of the screen. Select how you want to share it (e.g. email, Twitter, etc.).

Step 6: To delete the screenshot, right-click the picture and select Delete.

Step 7: To edit the screenshot, open the screenshot in an image editing program, such as Paint or Photoshop. There, you can crop, draw on, or otherwise modify the screenshot as needed.

3. How to Take a Screenshot on Microsoft Surface Laptop 4#

  1. To take a screenshot on your Surface Laptop 4, press and hold the Windows logo key and the volume down key at the same time.
  2. The screenshot will be saved automatically in the Screenshots folder located in the Pictures library.
  3. You can access the folder by clicking on the Start Menu and locating the Pictures folder.
  4. Inside the Pictures folder, you’ll find the Screenshots folder.
  5. Open the Screenshots folder to view the screenshot you just took.
  6. The file will be named as a PNG file with the current date and time of when the screenshot was taken.
