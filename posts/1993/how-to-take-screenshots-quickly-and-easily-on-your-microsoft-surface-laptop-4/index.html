1. Understanding the Basics of Taking Screenshots#

Taking a screenshot on your Surface Laptop 4 is easy. All you have to do is press and hold the Windows logo + Volume Down buttons at the same time until the screen dims. The screenshot image will then be automatically saved to your Pictures > Screenshots folder. To review your screenshot, press the Windows logo + Volume Up.

You can also access the screenshots you take by going to the Photos app on the Start Menu where you’ll find them stored in the ‘Screenshots’ folder. By opening a screenshot in this folder, you can easily share, print, or edit the image.

The screenshot you take can also be copied and pasted into other apps such as Word, PowerPoint, and Photoshop. All you have to do is press the Windows logo + Volume Up buttons and then press the copy icon. The screenshot will be temporarily stored on the clipboard and can then be pasted into any chosen app.

You can also take screenshots on the Surface Laptop 4 by using the ‘Print Screen’ button. To use this button, press and hold the ‘FN’ button and the ‘Print Screen’ button at the same time until the screen dims. The screenshot will be saved to the Pictures > Screenshots folder.

If you are using Windows 10, you can take a screenshot of the whole screen or of individual apps by pressing and holding the Alt+Print Screen keys at the same time. This will save the screenshot to the clipboard, which can then be pasted into any chosen app.

You can also take an screenshot of a single window by pressing and holding Alt + FN + Print Screen. This will take a screenshot of only the active window, which can then be saved or shared.

Using the above methods, taking screenshots on the Surface Laptop 4 is simple and straightforward.

2. Steps Required to Take a Screenshot#

Taking screenshots on a Microsoft Surface Laptop 4 is fast and easy. All it takes is a few simple steps. First, press and hold the Windows logo button + the Volume Down button at the same time until the screen dims. With this easy to follow guide, screenshots can be taken within seconds with the Microsoft Surface Laptop 4. The newly taken screenshots can be customized as per requirement. These screenshots can be modified by using the snipping tool. The snipping tool allows you to capture different kinds of screenshots, including rectangular, free-form, window, and full-screen snips. You can also draw and add text to your snips. Furthermore, this tool also allows you to save and share the screenshots.

The screenshots can also be cropped and edited using any photo-editing application. After the screenshot is edited, you can save it in various popular image formats such as JPEG, PNG, BMP, and GIF.

You can also add a watermark to your screenshots in order to protect them from being misused. You can add your company logo or copyright symbol as a watermark and save the screenshots for use. It is also possible to blur sensitive information like account details or passwords in the screenshot, to ensure privacy and reduce chances of misuse.

Microsoft Surface Laptop 4 also offers various other features to customize the screenshots. You can change the annotation color, add captions, and also undo the edits if necessary. Furthermore, you can also share the screenshots directly from the app.

4. Tips to Improve Your Screenshotting Experience#

  1. Make sure your Surface Laptop is unlocked so that pressing the Windows logo + Volume Down buttons will take a screenshot of the active screen.
  2. To take a screenshot of a specific window, click on it to make it the active window then press and hold the Windows logo + Volume Down buttons simultaneously to capture it.
  3. To take a screenshot of an entire page, press and hold the Windows logo + Volume Down button and press the spacebar at the same time.
  4. After taking the screenshot, the image will be automatically saved to the Pictures > Screenshots folder.
  5. To review the screenshot, press the Windows logo + Volume Up buttons simultaneously to pause the screen.
  6. If a notification appears at the top of the screen informing you that the screenshot has been saved, press the notification to open the screenshots folder.

Q. What is a screenshot? A. A screenshot is an image taken of the display on your laptop, computer, or mobile device. It captures whatever is being displayed on the screen at that time.

Q. How do I take a screenshot on my Microsoft Surface Laptop 4? A. On your Surface Laptop 4, press and hold down the “Windows + PrtScn” keys together. The screenshot will be saved in the “Screenshots” folder within your Pictures folder.

Q. What if I want to take a screenshot of only one window? A. You can take a screenshot of the active window by pressing and holding the “Alt + PrtScn” keys together. The screenshot will be saved in the “Screenshots” folder within your Pictures folder.

Q. How can I view the screenshot that I just took? A. The screenshot that you just took will be saved in the “Screenshots” folder within your Pictures folder. You can view the screenshot by opening the folder and selecting the screenshot.
